Scope Description

The USCG, under DHS, seeks contractor support to enhance financial management operations at the Financial Services Center (FSC) in Chesapeake, VA. The FSC is a shared service provider for USCG, TSA, and CWMD using Oracle Federal Financials (EBS). Scope spans three functional pillars:

PillarKey ActivitiesSystems
Accounting Services Execute financial transactions; general accounting & reporting; payment processing; FSC Operations Division administrative support G-Invoicing, Invoice Processing Platform (IPP), Oracle EBS
Internal Controls Develop & assess financial/IT business process controls; conduct testing, risk assessments; monitor & implement Corrective Action Plans (CAPs) FISCAM, OMB A-123
Property Management Review property acquisition/disposition docs; validate accounting treatment; enter property transactions into financial system; support 800+ field units USCG financial system of record

Labor Categories Required

Labor CategoryCredential Requirements
Project ManagerPMP certification preferred; financial management experience
Senior Analyst / SMERelevant degree + 8+ years; CPA or equivalent preferred
Senior AuditorCPA / CISA; 6+ years federal audit experience
Intermediate AuditorAccounting degree; 3–6 years federal audit experience
Junior AuditorAccounting degree; 0–3 years experience

  • Project plan within 10 business days of award; updates as required
  • Business Continuity Plan (BCP) within 60 days of award; updated annually
  • Monthly status reports summarizing work performed and progress
  • All deliverables reviewed and approved by COR before submission
  • 40-hour workweek during FSC operating hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM, M–F

Strategic Objective (Government)

Strengthen USCG's financial management capabilities by achieving a clean audit opinion, improving financial reporting accuracy, and ensuring compliance with internal controls and federal regulations. The FSC currently handles DHS-wide FSMS shared services and is under recurring audit scrutiny.
