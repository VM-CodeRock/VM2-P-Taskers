3. SEWP V Vehicle Analysis

NASA SEWP V is a Government-Wide Acquisition Contract (GWAC) for IT products and services with a $20B ceiling per contract. Awards are placed as task orders against prime SEWP V contracts — only holders of a SEWP V prime contract may compete for task orders. MuleSoft (Salesforce) products are sold through authorized SEWP V resellers.
