OMB M-26-10: New Federal IT Transparency & Acquisition Oversight Mandate Takes Effect
OMB Memorandum M-26-10 ("Reinforcing Transparency, Accountability, and Oversight of Federal Technology"), issued March 31, 2026, requires: (1) Agency CIOs to submit monthly IT contract approval reports to OMB beginning May 2026 through October 2026; (2) All new contracts and solicitations issued after March 31 to include provisions requiring IT vendors to disclose utilization rates and pricing data; (3) GSA will share model contract language for implementation. This directly affects every IT vendor — including Changeis — through new data-sharing provisions. Contractors should anticipate new CLIN structures and reporting obligations in upcoming task orders.
