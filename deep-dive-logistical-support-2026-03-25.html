02 Opportunity Overview

Full Requirement Description

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA), an agency modal within the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT), is seeking vendors capable of providing comprehensive logistical support at various FTA meetings and conferences, as detailed in an attached Statement of Objectives (SOO). This Sources Sought is a market research tool — no award will be made from capability information received — but it is a clear precursor to a formal RFP for a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA).

The contractor will be responsible for a full suite of meeting and conference logistics including: taking notes and providing meeting minutes; preparing meeting materials (name badges, table tents, binders, posters, CDs of materials); facilitating satisfaction surveys; coordinating hotel arrangements and invitational travel; providing and operating audio/visual (AV) equipment; setting up and dismantling equipment at least one hour before/after events; ensuring hotel/conference sites are booked at least six months in advance; and assisting with progress monitoring of all logistical aspects for each event.
