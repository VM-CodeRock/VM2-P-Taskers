Requirement Summary

This RFI is market research for a recompete of SEVP Program Management Office (PMO) Support — the successor to Changeis's current contract 70CTD022FR0000005. The PWS defines three coordinated contractor teams:

TeamFunctionKey Scope
SMU Team
System Management Unit		 Unit-level PMO support Portfolio roadmap, IMS maintenance, calendar management, strategic planning, business owner support, executive briefings
SEVIS Programs Team (SPS)
SEVIS Programs Section		 Section-level program & project support Requirements elicitation, backlog grooming, user acceptance testing, production support, risk management, Schedule mgmt, knowledge management, Tableau analytics support
SBAS Team
SEVP Business Automation Section		 Section-level program admin SEVPAMS library management, business process documentation, task management, technical writing, records management (NO dev/coding)

Key Requirements Features

  • Agile-driven PMO: All three teams use Agile methodologies — backlog grooming, sprint planning, Product Owner comms
  • Generative AI mandate: Contractor staff must demonstrate proficiency with DHSChat and ICE-approved AI tools for document production
  • Performance standards: 98% timely deliverable submission; 90% staffing minimum (monthly review); 14-day government acceptance cycle
  • Key personnel: 9 named roles — Program Manager, Project Manager (SPS), SBAS Lead PMA, SEVIS Lead Requirements Analyst, SEVIS Requirements Analysts, SBAS PMAs, Technical Writer/Editor, Functional Testers, PMA
  • Security: DHS 4300A/B compliance, PIV/FIPS 201, FISMA, ICE SLM Handbook — IT-2 / public trust clearances likely
  • SEVPAMS platform: SharePoint / Dynamics 365 / Power Apps / Azure / Tableau — contractor must be proficient
  • Exclusions: No software development, no coding, no SEVIS environment management for SBAS team
  • Demo requirement: Government will select respondents for live operational demonstrations — platforms and data services
