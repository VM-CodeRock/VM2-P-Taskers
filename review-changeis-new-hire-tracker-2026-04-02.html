⚠ High — John Moore on Indefinite Hold (FAA CLMRS)
Offer accepted November 14, 2025. Background cleared. No start date. 139+ days on hold with no documented reason or action. This candidate may be on competitor short list and risk walking without proactive engagement.
⚠ High — Tammy Quach Degree Waiver (FAA R&D)
Offer accepted March 4, 2026. Notes indicate "degree didn't align with LCAT, waiver not yet approved." 28 days without resolution. FAA R&D placements have long approval chains — this needs escalation to the contracting officer now.
⚠ Medium — IT SWAT Has 4 Unfilled Slots
3 Test Engineer positions (2× TE II, 1× TE I) are completely open with no committed candidate. Given FMCSA contract performance obligations, these gaps represent delivery risk if the contract requires full staffing within defined periods of performance.
⚠ Medium — 23 Candidates with No Client Assignment
14.8% of the pipeline has no client listed. These may be internal roles, pre-award contingent hires, or data entry gaps. Without client attribution, workload projections and contract performance reporting are incomplete.
⚠ Medium — 38.9% of Pipeline Failures Undocumented
14 of 36 failed hires have no reason code. This prevents root cause analysis and makes it impossible to differentiate controllable attrition (compensation, speed-to-offer) from uncontrollable losses (client budget cuts). Process improvement requires documented exit reasons.

Immediate: Resolve John Moore's Hold Status P1 — This Week

This FAA CLMRS candidate has been cleared and waiting 139+ days. A personal call from the hiring manager or president's office should determine whether the position is still live, the candidate is still available, and what is blocking activation. 3
Near-Term: Deputize a Backup to Jessica Underwood P1 — 30 Days

Cross-train at least one other recruiter or program manager to handle FAA CLMRS hiring. Document the FAA CLMRS process in a runbook. Consider splitting the portfolio so no single HM exceeds 25% of active pipeline. This is the most critical business continuity gap in the tracker.

4
Escalate Tammy Quach LCAT Waiver (FAA R&D) P1 — 5 Days

28 days without resolution on a qualification waiver indicates it has stalled at the CO or TPOC level. President should confirm whether a formal waiver request has been submitted, who is tracking it, and whether there is a fallback candidate if FAA denies.

5
Fill IT SWAT Test Engineer Gaps P2 — 30–60 Days

Three Test Engineer slots (2× TE II, 1× TE I) are completely unfilled. If FMCSA contract performance obligations require fully staffed teams, these gaps could result in contract deductions or escalations. Begin sourcing immediately through cleared talent pools. The URS Modernization pipeline has relevant contacts.

6
Require Exit Reason Codes for All Pipeline Failures P2 — Process Change

Mandate that any candidate marked as failed/lost (Start Date = "X") must have a documented reason in the Notes field before the record is closed. Create a standard taxonomy: Candidate Declined, Rescinded, Client Failed Resume, Position Eliminated, Clearance Issue, Accepted Competing Offer, Other. This enables quarterly attrition analysis and improves sourcing strategy.

7
Assign Client and Contract to All Active Records P2 — 2 Weeks

23 active records have no client assigned. At minimum, every active candidate should have a client (or "Pre-Award" / "Internal") and a contract number (or "TBD"). This is required for accurate contract-level headcount reporting to clients and for Changeis's own BD pipeline tracking.

8
Review Compensation Against Offer Acceptance Rate P3 — Quarterly

Changeis's offer acceptance rate of ~76% (those who accepted vs. were offered) is below the 80–90% industry benchmark. Post-offer decline tracking (4 declined, 3 withdrew, 1 took competing offer) suggests compensation or speed-to-close may be non-competitive, particularly for cleared candidates in a tight labor market. Consider a quarterly salary market review against ClearanceJobs and Salary.gov data for key labor categories.

9
Migrate Resume Tracking to Main New Hires Tab P3 — Process

The Resume Status column is blank for 91.6% of candidates in the primary hiring tab. While the ATAPS and URS staffing tabs have robust resume tracking, the main tab lacks this visibility. Either populate the Resume Status field for all active hires or add a hyperlink to the relevant staffing tab row so the New Hires tab serves as a true master record.
