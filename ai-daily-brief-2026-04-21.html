Meta Debuts Muse Spark — First Model from Meta Superintelligence Labs
April 8, 2026 (rolling out)
Meta
Meta unveiled Muse Spark, the first model from its new Meta Superintelligence Labs (led by Scale AI's Alexandr Wang after Meta's $14.3B investment). Use Case for Varun

Its health benchmark leadership is relevant if Changeis pursues VA or HHS contracts.
