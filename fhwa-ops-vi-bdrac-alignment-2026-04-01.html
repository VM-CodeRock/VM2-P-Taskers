April 1, 2026  ·  Changeis Confidential
1 · Executive Summary

The Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) Office of Operations (HOP) is procuring the OPS VI Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) as a follow-on to OPS V (expiring March 27, 2027). The opportunity spans 6 program areas over a 60-month base period, issued under the GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MRAS) vehicle, NAICS 541611.

Overall Assessment
MODERATE-STRONG Alignment  |  PURSUE WITH PARTNER

The BDRAC search returned 28 relevant task orders across Changeis's portfolio, including direct FHWA Office of Operations support (V3362009, best chunk score 0.706) and direct freight research support (V3272119). Changeis has demonstrated capabilities in program management, communications, outreach, data science, automation research, and work zone data — all of which map to OPS VI program areas.

However, Changeis lacks deep domain expertise in several specialized transportation engineering areas required by OPS VI: TransCAD traffic modeling, freight network analysis at scale, bridge strike analysis, weigh-in-motion programs, truck size and weight enforcement, and MUTCD-specific regulatory support. These gaps, combined with the presence of deep-domain incumbents (Battelle, Booz Allen Hamilton, Leidos, ICF, WSP), mean that a teaming arrangement is necessary to remain competitive.
