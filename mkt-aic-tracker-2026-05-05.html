Aviation Innovation Coalition
Active Owner: Varun Malhotra Managed via: Crossroads Strategies (Scott Brenner) Created: 2026-05-05 Updated: 2026-05-05
Executive Summary

The Aviation Innovation Coalition (AIC) is a newly formed industry group of Founding Members convened by Crossroads Strategies (Scott Brenner) to advance FAA ATC modernization through coordinated industry advocacy. Varun Malhotra / Changeis is a Founding Member.

Phase 1 Hill outreach concluded the week of April 8, 2026 with 6 congressional and agency meetings — receiving strong bipartisan support. Policymakers welcomed the AIC's coordinated voice and are eager for best-practice and technology performance data. DOT Under Secretary Ryan McCormack encouraged outreach to Secretary Duffy's Chief of Staff and the Deputy Secretary. A request to meet with FAA Administrator Bedford has been submitted.

Next steps: Board structure finalization, inaugural press release, member dues collection, and a follow-on meeting with FAA Administrator / DOT senior officials. Three time-sensitive action items are open for each Founding Member (Board rep, headshot/bio, invoicing contact).
