FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS SECTION

Section Headline:

Common Questions

Q: Do we need a change management program if we already have an IT implementation team?
A: Technology implementation and change management address entirely different problems. Implementation deploys the capability. Change management ensures people use it, trust it, and integrate it into their workflows. Organizations that rely on IT teams alone to drive adoption consistently report lower utilization, higher resistance, and weaker ROI. The two capabilities are complementary — and both are required.

Q: How does Changeis handle classified or sensitive federal environments?
A: Our team has experience navigating the security, clearance, and data classification requirements of federal environments. Our governance frameworks are designed to be adapted for sensitive contexts, and our consultants work within your security protocols.

Q: How long does an AI change management engagement typically take?
A: Assessment and Planning typically complete within 60-90 days. Pilot phases run 60-120 days. Scale and Sustain are ongoing capabilities, typically structured as 12-18 month programs with defined milestones and off-ramps. We design programs for your timeline and organizational velocity.

Q: We're a mid-size GovCon firm — is this framework right for us?
A: Yes. CAACMF is designed to scale from 50-person specialized firms to large federal agencies. The phases and tools adapt to your organizational size, and we size our engagement model accordingly.
