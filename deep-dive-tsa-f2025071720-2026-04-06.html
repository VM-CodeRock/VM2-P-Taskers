Requirement Summary (from APFS)

The Transportation Security Administration’s (TSA) office of Training and Development (T&D) is responsible for developing, delivering, managing, and measuring mission-critical training.  T&D’s Training Center Division (Atlantic City, NJ), Canine Training Center (San Antonio, TX) and Training Program Branch (Glynco,GA and Las Vegas, NV) have a continuing requirement for ongoing maintenance and technical support services for its existing Academy Administration Information System (ACADIS) Readiness Suite.  The vendor will provide ongoing maintenance and technical support for the following ACADIS modules and software: Training and Compliance Management, Automated Registration, Scheduling, Testing, Document Maintenance and ACADIS System Reports.
