Daily writing habit

A focused prompt for a govcon founder/leader · 2026-03-24
Timebox: 20 minutes

Prompt (2–3 sentences)

Write a 250–400 word “Operating Thesis” for Changeis for the next 90 days: what you will say “no” to, what you will double-down on, and the one measurable outcome that proves it worked. Anchor it in a real constraint (time, pipeline, people, or budget) so it reads like a decision, not an aspiration. Close with a single sentence you could repeat to your team every week.

Follow-up

List the top 3 commitments you must make this week to keep the thesis real (calendar blocks, customer conversations, partner outreach, proposal/capture moves). For each: owner, first step, and the date you’ll know it’s on track.

Note: This is designed to create clarity and trade-offs (not a vision statement). If you want, reply with your thesis and I’ll help tighten it into a one-page internal memo.
