1. Executive Summary

CBP's Office of Information Technology, Border Enforcement Management Systems (BEMS) directorate is forecasting a new small business set-aside BPA (FAR Part 8 or 13) worth $20M–$50M over a 5-year period (Jul 2026 – Jun 2031) to stand up and operate an Agile Program Management Office (APMO) with co-located Acquisition Support functions. The APMO will deliver comprehensive Service Delivery Management, IT Governance, and Technology Management for CBP/OIT/BEMS — the directorate responsible for the full lifecycle of border enforcement IT systems including design, development, deployment, and O&M.
