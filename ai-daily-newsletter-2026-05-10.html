Lead Story
Anthropic

Claude Goes Native in Microsoft 365 — Excel, Word, PowerPoint GA; Outlook Beta Launches

Anthropic officially released its Claude AI add-ins for Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Word as generally available on May 7, with Claude for Outlook simultaneously entering public beta. Available to Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers, the integration gives Claude agentic powers — not just Q&A — inside each app. In Excel, it debugs formulas, fills templates, and queries connected data sources. In PowerPoint, it builds full decks from scratch, generates charts, and iterates on slides while preserving brand formatting. In Word, it edits selected text, populates templates, and processes comment threads autonomously. In Outlook (beta), it triages unread mail, drafts replies in the user's voice, summarises long threads, reads attachments, schedules meetings, and produces one-page pre-meeting briefs. Enterprise admins can deploy all four add-ins from Microsoft AppSource via the M365 Admin Center. The integrations support Claude Opus 4.7, Opus 4.6, and Sonnet 4.6.

Executive Use Case Monday morning: Claude triages your Outlook inbox before you open it, surfaces the three emails requiring decisions, drafts two replies in your tone, and builds a one-page brief for your 9 AM kickoff — all before you take your first sip of coffee. For GovCon executives, the Word and PowerPoint integrations unlock compliant proposal formatting automation at enterprise scale.

OpenAI

GPT-5.5 Instant Becomes ChatGPT's New Default Model — Free Tier Included

OpenAI deployed GPT-5.5 Instant on May 5 as the replacement for GPT-5.3 Instant across all ChatGPT tiers, including the free tier. The new model scores 81.2 on AIME 2025 (vs 65.4 for its predecessor) and achieves 76 on MMMU-Pro multimodal reasoning. Its defining upgrade is context management: it can now use its search tool to reference past conversations, uploaded files, and Gmail to personalise answers. The model automatically chooses whether to invoke GPT-5.3 Instant or GPT-5.5 Thinking based on prompt complexity. GPT-5.5 Thinking and GPT-5.5 Pro remain paywalled for Plus/Pro/Business/Enterprise tiers. API pricing for the full GPT-5.5 is $5 per 1M input / $30 per 1M output tokens with a 1M context window.

Executive Use Case Executives on free or basic ChatGPT plans now get smarter, memory-aware responses without upgrading. For enterprise teams, the Gmail integration means chat assistants can now pull live email context to draft meeting agendas or board summaries on demand.
Sources: TechCrunch · OpenAI Request Deep Dive

Pentagon Clears 8 AI Firms for Classified Networks — "AI-First Fighting Force" Declared

The U.S. Department of Defense formally announced agreements with OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, NVIDIA, SpaceX, Amazon Web Services, Reflection, and Oracle to deploy frontier AI on its classified Impact Level 6 (Secret) and IL7 (Top Secret) networks. The DoD's GenAI.mil platform has already reached 1.3 million personnel and generated tens of millions of prompts with hundreds of thousands of agents deployed in just five months. The DoD explicitly cited anti-vendor-lock rationale for selecting a diverse vendor slate.

OpenAI

GPT-5.5-Cyber Previewed to Vetted Security Teams — Specialized for Vulnerability & Malware Work

OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.5-Cyber on May 7 — a specialised variant of GPT-5.5 optimised for cybersecurity workflows, available in limited preview to approved security teams. Unlike the standard model, it adopts a more permissive stance on security-related tasks including vulnerability discovery, triage, patch validation, and malware analysis — use cases where standard safety rails would otherwise block legitimate work. OpenAI stressed it is not designed to enhance offensive capabilities but to remove friction for defenders. White House and Congressional committees were briefed before launch. The release follows Anthropic's Mythos Preview (a model Anthropic withheld from release due to cybersecurity risk), signalling a bifurcation of frontier AI into standard and cleared-access tiers.

Executive Use Case (GovCon) CISO teams and cleared cybersecurity contractors should apply for early access. GPT-5.5-Cyber represents a potential step-change for vulnerability assessment, threat hunting, and red-team exercises on classified systems — aligning directly with the new DoD IL6/IL7 AI agreements.

Mistral

Mistral's Voxtral TTS Open Source — Edge-Ready, 5-Second Voice Cloning in 9 Languages

Mistral released Voxtral TTS, an open-source text-to-speech model based on Ministral 3B, designed for enterprise voice agents and edge deployment.
