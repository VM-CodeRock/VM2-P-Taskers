Original Instructions & Approach
Original Instructions
Build a filterable HTML deliverable of the 2026 FIFA World Cup games with filters for team, date (including weekday), and location. Include a quality/popularity rating for each game.
Approach
Researched full 104-match schedule from roadtrips.com (authoritative source with confirmed groups and fixtures). Applied a custom rating algorithm based on star teams, host nations, knockout stage floor ratings, weekend bonuses, and opening match bonus. Built a fully self-contained HTML/JS app with sticky filters, sortable columns, 5-star visuals, and light/dark theme toggle.
Key Findings
  • All 48 teams are now confirmed (draw finalized Dec 5, 2025; last qualifiers decided Mar 31, 2026).
  • 12 groups of 4, top 2 + 8 best 3rd-place advance to R32 — 104 total matches.
  • Knockout matchups from R32 onward use group-position placeholders as teams are not yet determined (tournament hasn't started).
  • Group stage runs June 11-27; Final at MetLife Stadium NJ on July 19.
  • Star power: Group J (Argentina/Algeria/Austria/Jordan), Group I (France/Senegal/Norway), Group C (Brazil/Morocco), Group L (England/Croatia) rated highest.
