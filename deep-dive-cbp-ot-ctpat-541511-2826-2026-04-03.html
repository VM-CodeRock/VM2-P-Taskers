Requirement Description

The objective is development, enhancements, and operations & maintenance (O&M) of the Customs Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (CTPAT) systems and Trade Compliance (TC) Portal. The primary goal is to provide CTPAT Portal O&M and enhancements required for evolving business, regulatory, IT security and system/software architectural advancement to CBP's Cargo Systems Program Directorate (CSPD) systems, including:

  • Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) — CBP's primary trade processing system/single window
  • CTPAT System — Voluntary supply chain security program portal (11,000+ member companies)
  • Trade Compliance (TC) Portals — Import/export compliance applications

The CTPAT program has seen active expansion following the CTPAT Pilot Program Act of 2023 (P.L. 118-98), which opened the program to freight brokers and 3PL warehouse facilities for the first time, driving portal development demand.
