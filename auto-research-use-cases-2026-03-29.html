What Is Auto Research / Agentic AI?
The 30-Second Explanation

Agentic AI (also called "auto research AI" or "autonomous AI") is the next evolution beyond chatbots. Instead of answering one question at a time, an AI agent can be given a goal — like "research all FAA contracts expiring in 90 days and identify Changeis pursuit opportunities" — and autonomously plan, search, analyze, and deliver a completed briefing without step-by-step human direction. You're already using this: Perplexity Computer is an agentic AI system.

The technology underpinning agentic AI includes: (1) frontier language models (Claude, GPT-5.4, Gemini 3.1) as the reasoning engine; (2) tools and integrations (MCP, APIs) for taking actions; (3) memory systems for continuity across tasks; and (4) multi-agent orchestration (multiple specialized agents collaborating on a single workflow). By March 2026, MCP has 97 million installs — meaning every serious AI tool now speaks the same "tool language," enabling seamless agent-to-agent workflows.
