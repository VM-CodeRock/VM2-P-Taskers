Requirement Description

CBP seeks a qualified contractor to provide administrative support personnel for its Office of Facilities and Asset Management (OFAM). Three core service domains are identified:

1. Executive & Administrative Support Managing calendars, travel coordination, meeting logistics, and official document management to streamline daily operations for senior OFAM leadership.
2. Communications & Knowledge Management Strategic messaging, content development, and management of information platforms — specifically SharePoint — to support effective internal and external coordination.
3. Financial & Budget Management Support Budget planning, execution, analysis, reporting, and acquisition support using SAP, BW, and TRIRIGA to improve financial stewardship and compliance.

Acquisition Strategy: Planned as OASIS+ solicitation, unrestricted. OASIS+ is GSA's government-wide multi-agency IDIQ for non-IT professional services — it replaced the legacy OASIS/OASIS SB vehicles (ordering periods closed March 1, 2025).
