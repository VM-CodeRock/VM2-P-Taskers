Regulatory Alert — MAS AI Clause & TDR
Action Required — MAS Refresh 31: GSA's proposed AI clause (GSAR 552.239-7001) — delayed from Refresh 31, expected in a future MAS refresh — will require all Schedule contractors to disclose AI systems used in contract performance within 30 days of award and notify GSA within 7 days of any material change to AI output bias or safety guardrails. Changeis VM2 agentic stack and any AI-augmented deliverables will fall under this disclosure requirement once finalized. Begin internal AI system inventory now.
  • TDR Mass Mod: 90-day acceptance window once issued. All 112 SINs now subject to quarterly transactional data reporting. Price Reductions Clause will be eliminated — Changeis no longer required to track commercial price parity for those SINs moving to TDR. Washington Technology coverage.
  • OLM SIN Addition: GSA strongly recommends adding the Order-Level Materials SIN to replace open-market item flexibility. Coordinate with Changeis contract admin to assess whether OLM SIN addition is needed.
  • AI Clause Comment: Industry comment deadline was April 3, 2026 (extended from March 20). Final clause implementation via later MAS refresh. Wiley Rein analysis.
