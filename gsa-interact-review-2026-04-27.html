AI · IT 📅 Mar 6, 2026 (draft); Extended comment deadline Apr 3, 2026 👤 GSA FAS

Draft GSAR Clause 552.239-7001 — AI System Safeguarding Requirements

GSA issued draft GSAR 552.239-7001 "Basic Safeguarding of Artificial Intelligence Systems" for public comment. The clause will be mandatory in all GSA solicitations and contracts for AI capabilities when finalized in Refresh 32. Key requirements: (1) Disclose all AI systems used in performance; (2) Use only "American AI Systems"; (3) Enable human oversight by government officials; (4) Report security incidents to CISA within 72 hours; (5) Daily status updates after incidents. The comment period closed April 3, 2026. This deferred from Refresh 31 — Refresh 32 implementation expected in H2 2026.

Action: Review draft clause at GSA.gov. Inventory all AI tools used in Changeis contract performance. Build AI disclosure readiness protocol now ahead of Refresh 32.
