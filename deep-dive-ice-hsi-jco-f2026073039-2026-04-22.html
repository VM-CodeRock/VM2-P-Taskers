4. JCO Role Analysis — What Does This Work Actually Entail?

The "Junior Compliance Officer" title is HSI-specific terminology for an immigration enforcement paraprofessional, not an IT compliance or management consulting analyst. Based on the SOW language and corroborated by a 2018 Hamilton Enterprises JCO job posting that uses nearly identical SOW language, the role entails:

JCO DutyDescriptionDomain
Form I-9 Audits Conduct Employment Eligibility Verification audits; maintain evidence of work authorization for audited employers Immigration Enforcement
DHS Database Queries Utilize DHS law enforcement databases to run queries, prepare reports, spreadsheets, and conduct research Law Enforcement Systems
Reports of Investigation Complete ROIs in HSI's case management system; document audit findings, noncompliance instances, unlawful employment practices Investigative Operations
Notice Preparation Prepare Notices of Intent to Fine, Warning Notices, and related documentation for HSI audits Legal/Regulatory Action
Business Records Review Systematic examination of employment documents, business records, government computer indices Document Review
Field Embedding Embedded with local HSI special agents; collaborative investigative support Law Enforcement Field Ops
Liaison Activities Support national/international investigative programs and liaison activities Investigative Support

Clearance Requirement: Personnel must be eligible for a DHS Public Trust clearance. Given the field-embedded, law enforcement nature of the work, background investigation timelines add hiring friction.

Skills Profile: Successful JCO candidates hold bachelor's degrees (or associates + 3 years experience), knowledge of immigration law and Form I-9 procedures, experience with government investigation or compliance, and strong written communication for report drafting. This is a specialized investigative paraprofessional profile — not a typical consulting analyst.

Key Assessment: The NAICS code 541611 (Management Consulting) is used for procurement vehicle purposes but does not reflect the actual nature of the work. This is investigative staff augmentation for a law enforcement agency. The practical skill set required is immigration-compliance auditing, not management consulting.
