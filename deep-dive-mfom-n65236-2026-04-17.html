Opportunity Description (from SAM.gov): This notice is regarding Market Survey N65236-26-RFPREQ-LSUBP00095-0008 which was released from 04 February 2026 through 20 February 2026. As a result of analysis of the responses submitted, Naval Information Warfare Center Atlantic has determined the requirement for Maintenance Figure of Merit (MFOM) System and Software Engineering Support will be solicited as 100% Small Business Set-Aside. The RFP is anticipated to be released Q4 FY2026 via PIEE and will be open to SeaPort NxG MAC Holders only.

Full Scope (per NIWC Atlantic Industry Forecast): The scope covers systems engineering, technical, and management support services for NIWC Atlantic in support of PEO MLB Logistics IT Services Delivery Team. This support encompasses enhancing and sustaining the MFOM Family of Systems (FoS) as well as transitioning and integrating MFOM's existing capabilities into PEO MLB LOG IT Services Delivery Team future FoS. The related systems are comprised of Tactical, Satellite, Terrestrial, and Ground Communications and Client Computing equipment with hardware and software elements. Support encompasses sustainment through Engineering Change Proposals (ECPs) via systems engineering, integration, testing, configuration management, quality control, logistics, training, and fielding support.
