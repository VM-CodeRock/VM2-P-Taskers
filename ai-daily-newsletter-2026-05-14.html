Anthropic Launches Claude for Small Business — 10-City Tour Kicks Off in Chicago Today

Anthropic announced Claude for Small Business on May 13, a bundled suite of connectors and ready-to-run workflows built for the 36 million U.S. small businesses that make up the economy's backbone.
