2. LTL Studios — Vendor Profile

Company Overview

LTL Studios (ltlstudios.co) is a premium live-learning delivery partner — not a content platform or off-the-shelf training vendor. Founded in January 2025, they are co-founded by Will Mannon (previously co-founded Write of Passage with David Perell, 200-person cohorts, multiple seasons) and operate under the tagline "Not a platform. A delivery partner."

Mission & Positioning

LTL Studios designs, builds, and runs premium live cohort programs for creators and "next-gen institutions" — end to end. Their value proposition is acting as an operations and curriculum partner who handles everything from validate → set up → deliver → scale. They work on a revenue-share basis, aligning incentives with client outcomes.

Pricing

LTL Studios operates on a revenue-share model — no fixed per-seat or retainer pricing is published. This is standard for program-build partnerships. Estimated range for a bespoke corporate cohort build: $15,000–$50,000+ program setup + revenue share, based on comparable premium cohort-build partners. Direct quote required.

AI-Specific Capabilities

LTL Studios uses AI in their operations layer (surfacing patterns, personalizing experiences, smarter operational decisions) but their client-facing programs are not AI-training-specific unless the partner (like Every) brings AI content. Their Every Claude Code workshops demonstrate ability to deliver AI-adjacent training — but the AI content originates with the partner, not LTL Studios.

GovCon Fit Assessment

Verdict: LTL Studios is INDIRECT FIT. They are a best-in-class cohort delivery partner for building a bespoke "Changeis AI Academy" — but not a vendor you buy off-the-shelf. They require 2–4 months to build a program and are ideal for the long-term vision of Changeis owning its internal AI training brand. Not suited for near-term workforce upskilling at scale.

3. Comparable Vendors — Landscape Overview

3.1 Section AI (Section School)

Website: sectionai.com | Founded: 2019 (as Section School, rebranded to Section AI) | CEO: Greg Shove

Section AI is an enterprise AI training and transformation platform that has trained 15,000+ knowledge workers across Fortune 500 and mid-size firms. They offer workshops, async sprints, bootcamps, and a dedicated AI coach (ProfAI). In 2025, they pivoted from cohort-only to a full "AI transformation" suite including change management advisory and workflow automation services.

Delivery: Workshops (live, leadership-focused), AI Academy (async on-demand), custom live sessions, bootcamps, transformation sprints (8-week workflow redesign). Strong enterprise support: dedicated engagement manager, LMS integration, SSO, admin analytics, custom learning paths.

Pricing: $750/seat (teams <100) · Custom for 100+ (includes dedicated manager, custom sessions) · AI Transformation Services: custom quote

GovCon fit: No FedRAMP authorization disclosed, but suitable for unclassified work. Role-based curricula (BD, ops, finance, marketing, PM) align well. Change management advisory addresses GovCon adoption barriers.

3.2 OST Global Solutions — AI for Government BD

Website: ostglobalsolutions.com | Instructor: Olessia Smotrova, CF.APMP Fellow

OST Global Solutions is a GovCon-native BD and proposal training firm that launched an AI-specific course: Master AI to Enhance BD, Capture, & Proposal Processes. The instructor has led winning bids for four of the top five federal contractors, winning $26B+ over her career. This is the only vendor in this comparison built specifically for federal contractors' BD and proposal workflows.

Delivery: 3-part live virtual course (3 × 2.5-hour sessions via Zoom). Sessions recorded. Hands-on AI exercises included. Also available on-demand.

Pricing: $795/person (live virtual) — extraordinary value for GovCon-specific AI BD training

ROI claims: 30% productivity increase · 20% higher win rates · 25% labor cost savings in bid prep

GovCon fit: HIGHEST — built exclusively for federal contractors. Covers AI security and IP protection for competition-sensitive data. GSA Schedule pricing available ($1,511/participant via GSA Advantage for related Govini courses).

3.3 Reforge

Website: reforge.com

Reforge is a premium product & growth learning platform used by elite tech companies. They offer AI-specific courses (AI Foundations, AI Strategy, AI Growth, AI Product Leadership, AI Productivity) via live cohorts (Spring/Fall seasons) and on-demand. Content is created by operators from top tech firms (Google, Meta, Airbnb, etc.).

Delivery: Live cohort (seasonal: Spring + Fall, 3–4 weeks each) + on-demand library (40+ courses). Team plans include shared language, tracking, and custom learning paths. SSO + SCIM security.

Pricing: Individual: $1,995/yr · Starter (10 seats): $9,995/yr · Scale (30 seats): $23,995/yr · Enterprise (30+): custom (typically $30,000–$150,000+/yr)

GovCon fit: MODERATE — excellent for technical and product roles; less relevant for BD/capture/proposal GovCon workflows. Better suited for Changeis tech PMs and analysts than proposal writers or BD leads.

3.4 Coursera for Business

Website: coursera.org/business

Coursera for Business offers 10,600+ courses from top universities (Stanford, Google, IBM, Microsoft, Amazon). Strong GenAI track (Google AI Essentials, IBM Generative AI, Stanford ML). Academies for GenAI, Data, and IT. Interactive AI coach for personalized guidance. Used by federal agencies (GSA AI Community of Practice program used Coursera-adjacent higher-ed content).

Delivery: Async on-demand + structured learning paths + professional certificates. Team (5–125 users) + Enterprise (125+). AI-assisted Course Builder for custom content.

Pricing: Teams: ~$399/user/yr (5–125 users) · Enterprise: custom (~$250–$450/user/yr at scale, per Vendr benchmarks)

GovCon fit: MODERATE — broad AI fluency, excellent for foundational upskilling at scale. Not GovCon-specific. Good for baseline training before specialized OST/Section programs.

3.5 Deloitte — AI Academy & Government AI Training Framework

Website: deloitte.com/ai-academy

Deloitte's AI Academy (internal) and Government AI Training Framework (external advisory) represent the enterprise/consulting tier. They authored the Tailored AI Training for Government framework and have deployed AI training for GSA (14,000 participants, 200 government organizations, 92% satisfaction), UK Civil Service (One Big Thing initiative), and US Dept. of Interior. Their approach uses a tiered model: use → choose → build AI, calibrated by role, seniority, and computer use.

Delivery: Custom consulting engagement, workshop design, in-person + async video. Not a self-serve platform.

Pricing: Custom consulting rates — estimated $150,000–$500,000+ for an enterprise engagement. Accessible for large GovCon firms; significant for 150–300 person firm without pre-existing Deloitte relationship.

GovCon fit: HIGH for methodology — their government AI training framework is directly applicable. Cost is prohibitive for Changeis scale unless bundled with existing advisory work.
