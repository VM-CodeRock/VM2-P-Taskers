Scope Description (from APFS)

The new requirement has three defined work pillars:

Pillar 1: Executive & Administrative Support

Managing calendars, travel, meetings, and official documents to streamline senior leadership daily operations. Direct executive assistant support for OFAM leadership.

Pillar 2: Communications & Knowledge Management

Strategic messaging, content development, and management of information platforms like SharePoint for internal/external coordination. Stakeholder engagement strategies.

Pillar 3: Financial & Budget Management Support

Planning, execution, analysis, reporting, and acquisition support using SAP, BW, and TRIRIGA to improve financial stewardship and compliance. Capital Facilities Investment Plan (CFIP), RAP, FYHSP, UFR development.

OASIS+ (One Acquisition Solution for Integrated Services Plus) is GSA's consolidated contract vehicle for non-IT professional services. It replaced the legacy OASIS, HCaTs, and BMO vehicles. CBP's solicitation for OFAM FO Administrative Support will be placed under this vehicle via GSA eBuy as a Fair Opportunity task order.
