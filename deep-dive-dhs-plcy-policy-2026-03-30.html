Policy Context — Trump Administration Priorities

The DHS Office of Policy operates at the intersection of all six major Administration priority areas currently driving heightened PLCY activity. A winning proposal must explicitly align to these priorities:

  • Immigration Enforcement & Screening: Trump's January 2025 "enhanced vetting" EO directs DHS to implement heightened screening and vetting for all foreign nationals. PLCY serves as the policy coordination hub — Task Areas 1 and 2 are directly implicated.
  • Administration Alignment / DOGE: PLCY must align all DHS components to Administration priorities under OMB/DOGE guidance. Task Area 1 (Administration Alignment) is the highest-priority workstream under current DHS leadership.
  • Data-Driven Government: OMB M-25-21 directs evidence-based decision-making across all agencies. Task Area 2 (Data Analytics) is the execution mechanism for PLCY's performance reporting obligations.
  • Operational Efficiency: DOGE-era scrutiny on contractor overhead demands streamlined workflows, automation, and demonstrable efficiency gains — Task Areas 3 and 4.
  • Executive Correspondence Quality: DHS leadership turnover and high-tempo policy actions require robust ExecSec support (Task Area 6) for congressional correspondence, interagency taskings, and Secretary-level briefings.
