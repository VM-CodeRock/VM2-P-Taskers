Claude Sonnet 4.6 Tops ClawBench at 33.3% on 144 Live Production Websites

Researchers from UBC and Vector Institute published ClawBench, a 153-task evaluation framework running on 144 real production websites across 15 categories (purchases, job applications, appointment booking).
