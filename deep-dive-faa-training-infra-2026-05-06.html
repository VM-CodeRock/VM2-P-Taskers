Requirement Summary

The FAA requires a contractor to maintain, repair, and support training systems and equipment used by the NAS Academy and its partners at MMAC. The Academy's mission is to deliver mission-ready FAA employees through technical training across air traffic control, technical operations, and aviation community programs.


Nine defined task areas:

Task Area Description
Task 1 Contract Management Program management, reporting, staffing oversight, COR coordination
Task 2 EnRoute Automation Maintain/repair ERAM, HOST, MEARTS, DSR simulation systems in labs
Task 3 Terminal Automation ARTS/STARS, ACEPS Type II, DSR terminal sim support
Task 4 Radar CARSR, ARSR-4, radar training simulator O&M
Task 5 Communication VHF/UHF comm systems, NADIN, SIGNAL, training environment comms
Task 6 Navigation WAAS, ILS, VOR navigation system training support
Task 7 Environmental ESS, environmental/occupational safety systems in training buildings
Task 8 Emerging Technologies NextGen systems integration into training environments; new acquisitions
Task 9 IT Developer Software development, configuration management, IT infrastructure support

Key constraint: Contractor personnel must maintain a physical, on-site presence at MMAC during the full contract term. MMAC spans 445,398 sq ft of Academy space across 49 buildings. Off-site support limited to 50-mile radius of Oklahoma City metro.

Source: FAA Academy Training Systems Division Brief (2022) · HigherGov Opportunity Details
