Daily writing habit — 2026-03-23

Prompt (2–3 sentences)

Write a short “positioning memo” for Changeis: in one paragraph, explain why a federal program office should trust a small firm to deliver mission-critical modernization (focus on risk reduction, delivery credibility, and stakeholder management rather than buzzwords). Then add one sentence that contrasts your approach with a large integrator—without naming any company.

Follow-up

List 3 proof points you can cite today (past performance, named leaders, delivery artifacts, measurable outcomes). For each, note what public evidence you could point to (case study, CPARS excerpt, public award, partner reference).

  • Proof point #1: … (Evidence: …)
  • Proof point #2: … (Evidence: …)
  • Proof point #3: … (Evidence: …)
Tone: confident, specific, non-hype Timebox: 12 minutes
