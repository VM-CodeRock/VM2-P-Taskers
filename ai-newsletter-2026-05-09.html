Connecticut Legislature — Transparency Coalition, May 8

Connecticut AI Responsibility and Transparency Act (SB 5) Passed — Nation's Most Comprehensive State AI Law

Connecticut's legislature passed SB 5 (131-17 in the House, 32-4 in the Senate), now awaiting Governor Lamont's expected signature. The Connecticut Artificial Intelligence Responsibility and Transparency Act requires consumer disclosures for subscription AI products, whistleblower protections for frontier AI developer employees, safety protocols for AI chatbots (especially for minors), and labeling of AI-generated material. A regulatory sandbox provision was included to balance innovation.

Why It Matters — Varun

If Changeis or clients operate in Connecticut, SB 5 compliance becomes mandatory. The whistleblower and disclosure provisions affect AI vendor relationships—any AI tool embedded in client-facing services will need auditable disclosure documentation.
