Client Context: CBP Passenger Systems Program Directorate (PSPD)

PSPD is the primary OIT directorate supporting traveler and immigration management systems (TPVS suite, TECS). OMB M-25-21 (Feb 2025) mandates agencies accelerate responsible AI adoption — ADAPT is a direct acquisition response to that mandate.
