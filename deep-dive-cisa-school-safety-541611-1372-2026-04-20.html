⚠ NLR Status — Strategic Watch Required

This requirement is flagged "No Longer Required" (NLR) on the current APFS record, meaning the procurement was cancelled or deferred. However, a companion bridge contract (F2026072735, up to $2M) was posted in January 2026, and a full recompete remains expected for Q3 FY2026. Changeis should monitor and position for the recompete.

CISA's School Safety Task Force (SSTF) is a congressionally mandated program under the Bipartisan Safer Communities Act (BCSA) and the Luke & Alex School Safety Act (LASSA) of 2022. CISA administers the Federal School Safety Clearinghouse (SchoolSafety.gov) on behalf of four cabinet agencies (DHS, ED, DOJ, HHS) and delivers 13 critical national school safety functions to support 130,000+ schools.

The incumbent MetaPhase Consulting LLC won the prior $8.7M task order (70RCSJ23FR0000053) in September 2023, beating four competitors. The recompete is expected at $10M–$20M with a 5-year period of performance (Sep 2026–Sep 2031). The RAG system scored Changeis at 0.6667 (STRONG) — driven by 16+ matching chunks across safety program support, communications, stakeholder engagement, and SLTT capacity-building work from the FMCSA/Volpe portfolio.

The core work scope includes: strategic communications, stakeholder outreach, content development, website support (SchoolSafety.gov), social media management, product/tool development, training delivery, and inter-agency coordination — all areas where Changeis has demonstrated federal capability.
