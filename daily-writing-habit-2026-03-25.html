Daily Writing Habit

Date: 2026-03-25 · Audience: GovCon founder/leader
Today’s prompt

AI adoption is change management, not software

Write a short “point of view” memo on why federal agencies should treat AI adoption as a change-management program—not a software installation. Anchor it in one concrete mission outcome (e.g., faster grants processing, safer airspace, reduced improper payments) and name the single biggest behavior change required from frontline teams.

Follow-up

What would you measure in the first 30 days to prove momentum (not just activity)—and who owns those metrics?

Tip: Aim for 200–350 words. End with a single, specific ask you’d make to an agency executive sponsor.
