Competitive Landscape Summary

Critical Dynamic: OASIS SB Expiration Benefits Changeis

The legacy OASIS SB contract expired in December 2025. Metaphase (current ORS incumbent on OASIS SB) must now obtain OASIS+ UR or team with an UR prime to compete on this unrestricted requirement. This structural change significantly reduces Metaphase's competitive position unless they have already obtained OASIS+ UR on their own.

The unrestricted OASIS+ vehicle is the most important differentiator. Most historical OFAM contractors competed on small-business set-aside OASIS tracks. By going unrestricted, CBP opens this to all OASIS+ UR holders — potentially including large firms but also all SB-track holders if they hold a UR contract. Changeis holds OASIS+ UR (47QRCA25DU072), making it eligible without any teaming requirement for vehicle access.

The highest-threat competitor is Metaphase Consulting, whose current ORS contract covers front office management and centralized administrative support — nearly identical to the new requirement's scope. However, Metaphase may need a large business teaming partner or an OASIS+ UR prime to compete.
