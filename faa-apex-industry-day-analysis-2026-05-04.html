Strategic Context — Why This Matters for Changeis

APEX (Air Traffic Performance EXcellence System) is the FAA's training intelligence platform for the Academy. Per the SAM notice and the CAMI white paper referenced in the RFI, APEX targets three priorities:

  • Strengthen performance measurement — structured, consistent, reliable evaluations aligned to clear learning objectives.
  • Standardize readiness signals beyond pass/fail — a comprehensive view of trainee capabilities and support needs.
  • Close the field-to-Academy feedback loop — use downstream field performance data to inform upstream Academy curriculum and design.

This is the supply-side enabler of the FAA's controller-pipeline rebuild (3,500 below target, 2% applicant-to-cert conversion, $95M FY26 training budget). Industry Day = the agency's first chance to socialize what they heard from the RFI and shape what becomes the actual SOW.

Connection to NavTac II TO-16: Per topics.json signal added today, the new APEX task order is positioned to replace Tetra Tech NavTac II TO-16. That makes the May 15 event the de facto pre-RFP for a recompete with a known incumbent to displace.
