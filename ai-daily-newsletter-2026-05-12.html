  • "NanoResearch: Co-Evolving Skills, Memory, and Policy for Personalized Research Automation" — Xu et al. — 40-page system enabling AI agents to self-improve research workflows through co-evolution of skills, episodic memory, and policy. arXiv:2605.10813
