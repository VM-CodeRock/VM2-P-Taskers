Executive Summary

APFS record *F2025071528 represents a follow-on recompete for CISA's Training and Education System Support (TESS) contract — a Firm Fixed Price, Small Business set-aside requirement under NAICS 541611 administered through the CISA Office of the Chief Human Capital Officer (OCHCO). The work centers on monitoring and managing CISA's agency-wide training and education programs, distributing educational resources, and maintaining the agency's training management system.

The incumbent, KnowledgeBank, Inc., holds contract 70RCSA22FR0000069 (awarded 9/29/2022, value $18.7M, vehicle: HCaTS SB). Critically, KnowledgeBank has publicly indicated it no longer qualifies as a Small Business under NAICS 541612 — creating a structural vulnerability in its ability to compete on the SB set-aside recompete. The new contract will be on OASIS+ (not HCaTS), reflecting a vehicle transition that opens the field to OASIS+ SB awardees.

Changeis's RAG engine returned a STRONG 0.663 composite score against 17 past-performance matches, led by three DOT/FAA/Volpe Centerwide Training task orders with near-identical scope: training budget monitoring, LMS administration, training logistics, and resource distribution. The capability alignment is direct and substantive. Changeis should pursue this opportunity as a prime contractor, with early positioning through the OCHCO POC (Morgan Skaggs) before the May 2026 solicitation release.

Strategic Opportunity Window: With the incumbent size-standard issue and the vehicle transition to OASIS+, this is one of the highest-openness recompetes in the current CISA pipeline. KnowledgeBank may face disqualification or be forced to compete on OASIS+ Unrestricted — removing its incumbency advantage on the SB set-aside.
