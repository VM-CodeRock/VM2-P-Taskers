⚠ Watch Item — SAIC Late Addition

SAIC (693KA925D00008) was added to ATEPS-II in July 2025 — approximately 7 months after the initial December 2024 award cohort. FAA posted the SAM.gov notice on or around July 10, 2025. SAIC had at least two prior ODRA protest actions (ODRA-24-958 in September 2024 and ODRA-25-965 in March 2025). The causal link between these protests and SAIC's late addition to ATEPS-II has not been publicly confirmed, but the timing is notable. If ODRA-25-965 resulted in a corrective action directing FAA to add SAIC, any remaining unsuccessful offerors may have standing to file follow-on protests.

  • Docket: 25-ODRA-00976
  • Matter: Protest of Science Applications International Corporation
  • Date Served: March 21, 2025

Analysis — Probable Scenario

The sequence of events — SAIC protest (Sep 2024), second SAIC protest (Mar 2025 Final Order), followed by SAIC's late addition to ATEPS-II (Jul 2025) — is consistent with a corrective action pattern. In FAA ODRA proceedings, a sustained protest (or negotiated corrective action via ADR) can direct the agency to reconsider award decisions or extend award to additional offerors. SAIC's incumbent status on prior ATEPS contracts further supports the corrective-action hypothesis.

  • The full text of ODRA-25-965 Final Order is available at FAA ODRA Recent Cases (PDF)
  • Changeis and other original awardees should review whether any conditions in the Final Order affect ongoing task order competitions
  • Recommended action: Obtain and review the public version of ODRA-25-965 to confirm whether corrective action language applies to pending task orders
ODRA Regulatory Update — Pending Rule Change

The FAA is in the process of modernizing 14 CFR Part 17, which governs ODRA procedures. Key timeline:

  • NPRM: Planned for December 2025 (may be delayed)
  • Comment Period Close: February 2026 (estimated)
  • Scope: Modernize, reorganize, and clarify protest/dispute procedures — first revision since 2011
  • Contact: Diana Rabinowitz, FAA AGC, diana.rabinowitz@faa.gov
Implication for Changeis

Revised ODRA procedural rules could affect how task order protests are handled on ATEPS-II. Changeis should monitor the NPRM docket (RIN 2120-AM02) for any changes to protest standing, deadlines, or ADR requirements that could affect how Changeis defends task order awards or challenges adverse decisions.
