Today’s writing prompt
Write a short vignette about a moment when you realized a federal customer was really buying risk reduction—not just labor hours. Describe the signal you noticed (a question they asked, a concern in a meeting, a change in evaluation language), what you changed in your approach, and the measurable outcome.
Follow-up: What is one sentence you can add to your next capability statement or proposal to make that risk-reduction promise explicit?
Tip: Set a 12-minute timer. Write fast; revise later.
