Regulatory Watch: GSA AI Clause (GSAR 552.239-7001)

GSA's proposed AI safeguarding clause was pulled from MAS Refresh 31 and deferred to Refresh 32 after industry pushback. The comment period closed April 3, 2026. Key provisions Changeis must prepare for:

  • Disclosure: All AI systems used in contract performance must be disclosed within 30 days of award
  • Data ownership: Government retains full ownership of all data inputs, outputs, and custom developments
  • "American AI Systems": Restricts use of non-US AI platforms (definition currently ambiguous)
  • Service provider liability: Prime contractors responsible for AI subcontractor/service provider compliance — even non-subcontractors
  • Incident reporting: 72-hour reporting requirement for AI system incidents
  • Change management: 30-day concurrent access required before replacing major AI model versions
  • Acceptance window: 60 days from Refresh 32 issuance (shorter than standard 90 days)
Changeis action: Inventory all AI tools used in current contract performance. Engage legal counsel on "American AI Systems" definition. Monitor Refresh 32 timeline — estimated mid-2026 based on Refresh 31 delayed to late April. Sources: Holland & Knight analysis, Winvale analysis.
