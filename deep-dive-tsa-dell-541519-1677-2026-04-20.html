Requirement description (verbatim): "The contractor shall provide recurring maintenance and support for Dell-brand servers deployed through the TSA IT infrastructure. The support types required are End User ProSupport Mission Critical level of service as well as Post Standard Support."

Key interpretation: Two distinct Dell support tiers are in scope: (1) ProSupport Mission Critical — the highest Dell service level, featuring 4-hour on-site response 24x7x365, proactive monitoring via Dell AIOps/Secure Connect Gateway, and dedicated TAM access; and (2) Post Standard Support — covering hardware past its standard warranty/OEM support window (post-EoL/EoS assets). The contractor must hold an active GSA MAS contract under NAICS 541519 and be an authorized Dell reseller or partner.
