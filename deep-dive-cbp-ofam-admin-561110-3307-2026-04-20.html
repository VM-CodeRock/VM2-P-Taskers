Scope Summary: CBP seeks contractor personnel to support OFAM's Front Office across three functional pillars:

  • Executive & Administrative Support — Calendar management, travel coordination, meeting logistics, official document management for senior OFAM leadership
  • Communications & Knowledge Management — Strategic messaging, content development, SharePoint platform management, internal/external coordination support
  • Financial & Budget Management Support — Budget planning, execution, analysis, and reporting; acquisition support using SAP, BW (Business Warehouse), and TRIRIGA (IBM facility/real property management system)
Key systems: SAP (financial ERP), BW (budget analytics/reporting), and TRIRIGA (integrated workplace management — real property, equipment, projects). Changeis staff on the HCPS contract have direct hands-on experience with all three, providing a concrete technical differentiator.
