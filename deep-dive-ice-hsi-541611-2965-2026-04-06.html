Requirement Description

This requirement covers Records and Information Management (RIM) support across all of ICE's Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP). The work spans two interconnected systems:


System 1

SEVPAMS

  • SEVP Automated Management System — document repository & electronic records management for SEVP workflows
  • Data, document & records control
  • Electronic submissions handling
  • Must integrate with SEVPAMS support teams
System 2

ERMS / CollabWare

  • ICE-wide Electronic Records Management System
  • Runs on Collabware's Collabspace platform (FedRAMP Moderate JAB P-ATO; ICE ATO holder)
  • Administrative & configuration support
  • NARA-compliant lifecycle management
Mission Goals

SEVP RIM Objectives

  • Promote transparency & accountability via documentation
  • Move to 100% electronic environment
  • Implement full-text search for all artifacts
  • Apply metadata for proper records disposal per file plan
  • Operational & cost efficiencies

Key technical context: SEVPAMS is a SharePoint-based system that serves as ICE SEVP's primary document and workflow management platform. It maintains records for school certification, student tracking, compliance documentation, and the SEVIS interconnection. The ERMS (CollabWare) provides agency-level records lifecycle governance — automated categorization, retention, disposition certificates — layered on top of M365/SharePoint infrastructure. The contractor must bridge operational records management (SEVPAMS) with enterprise compliance (ERMS/CollabWare).

ICE / SEVP Mission Context

SEVP is the ICE program that manages the Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS), the federal database tracking approximately 1.5 million F/M/J nonimmigrant students and exchange visitors at ~10,000 certified schools. SEVP's records management obligations are extensive:


  • SEVPAMS: SharePoint-based workflow system that manages school certification, Form I-17 petitions, correction requests, and inquiry tracking. Interconnected with SEVIS in near-real-time.
  • Volume: SEVP processes millions of transactions annually — school petitions, compliance events, document submissions, appeal workflows.
  • NARA Mandates: Presidential Directives M-19-21 and M-23-07 require federal agencies to transition to fully electronic records and comply with NARA UERM (Universal Electronic Records Management) requirements by defined milestones.
  • CollabWare ATO: ICE holds an Agency-level FedRAMP Authority to Operate (ATO) for Collabspace, Collabware's SaaS platform. This is the foundation of the ERMS layer — the contractor supports its configuration and administration.
  • Data sensitivity: SEVP records contain PII for foreign nationals, school officials, and federal staff. The contractor must maintain DHS security posture including likely DHS Entry On Duty (EOD) suitability.
Strategic context (2025–2026): ICE enforcement activity has surged under current administration priorities. SEVP is simultaneously under pressure to (1) increase student tracking and school compliance enforcement and (2) meet NARA digital records mandates. This creates genuine demand for both operational RIM support (more transactions) and system modernization (100% electronic goal). The $5–10M range — nearly 3x the prior $3.43M contract ceiling — signals meaningful scope expansion.
