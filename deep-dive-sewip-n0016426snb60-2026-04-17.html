Program Background — SEWIP Block 3

The Surface Electronic Warfare Improvement Program (SEWIP) Block 3 is the Navy's third-generation upgrade to the AN/SLQ-32 shipboard electronic warfare suite. It adds a wideband active electronically scanned array Electronic Attack capability (using Gallium Nitride transmit/receive modules) to the Block 2 Electronic Support architecture, creating the AN/SLQ-32(V)7 configuration.
