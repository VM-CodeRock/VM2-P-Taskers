AI Change Management — Graphics Spec

Design specifications for CAACMF visual assets and social graphics

Changeis — AI Adoption Change Management

Graphics Specifications for Design Production

Brand Context:
- Company: Changeis, Inc. | Arlington, VA
- Tagline: "Aligning people, processes and technology across your entire enterprise"
- Service Context: Management consulting + large-scale IT solutions for government and professional services
- Tone: Authoritative, structured, modern, trustworthy — not startup-flashy; not legacy-stodgy
- Primary Audience: Federal agency leaders, GovCon executives, professional services decision-makers

BRAND PALETTE GUIDANCE

Note to designer: If Changeis has existing brand guidelines, these should override the suggestions below. The following palette is derived from what is appropriate for a federal/professional services consulting firm.

Primary Colors:
- Deep Navy Blue: #0D2B5E (authority, federal credibility, trust)
- Changeis Blue (accent): #1A5FA8 (primary action color, lighter blue variant)
- White: #FFFFFF (clean space, clarity)

Secondary / Accent Colors:
- Slate Gray: #4A5568 (supporting text, secondary elements)
- Light Blue Tint: #E8F0FE (background tints, cards, infographic backgrounds)
- Amber/Gold: #F5A623 (highlight accent, callout boxes, milestone markers — use sparingly)
- Emerald Green: #27AE60 (success metrics, positive indicators, check marks)
- Warm Red: #E53E3E (warnings, pitfalls, risk indicators — use minimally)

Typography:
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold, or Montserrat Bold)
- Subheads: Semi-bold sans-serif (Inter SemiBold)
- Body: Regular sans-serif (Inter Regular)
- Accent/Data: Tabular mono or similar for statistics (Inter Tabular Numerals if available)
- Avoid serif fonts for this audience context; clean and modern reads as more credible in tech-adjacent consulting

Grid/Layout Philosophy:
- Clean, structured, ample white space
- Data and text never compete — generous margins
- Icons: outline style, not filled/solid blocks; 2px stroke weight
- Photography: When used, prefer real humans in professional settings (no stock-photo clichés); aerial cityscapes for federal/DC context

GRAPHIC 1: Framework Overview Diagram

"The Changeis AI Adoption Change Management Framework (CAACMF)"

Purpose: Hero diagram for the website service page, downloadable framework brief cover, and LinkedIn campaign posts. The single most important visual asset — should function as a stand-alone explainer.

Dimensions:
- Web/Digital: 1600px × 900px (16:9, landscape)
- Print/PDF: 11" × 8.5" at 300 DPI (landscape orientation)
- LinkedIn Post version: 1200px × 628px (cropped/adapted)

Layout Description:
The diagram uses a horizontal flow architecture representing the five phases of CAACMF, with supporting layers above and below.

COMPOSITION STRUCTURE:

[TOP BAND — 80px height]
Title: "The Changeis AI Adoption Change Management Framework" | Subtitle: "Aligning People, Processes & Technology for Sustainable AI Transformation"
Background: Deep Navy (#0D2B5E) | Text: White

[MAIN CONTENT AREA — center, ~600px height]

CENTER ROW — Five Phase Blocks in horizontal flow:
[ASSESS] → [PLAN] → [PILOT] → [SCALE] → [SUSTAIN]

Each phase block:
- Shape: Rounded rectangle, 220px wide × 280px tall
- Background: White with 2px navy border
- Top: Phase number (01, 02, 03, 04, 05) in large amber numeral (#F5A623), 36pt
- Phase name in Deep Navy, 22pt bold
- Icon below name: Phase-specific outline icon (see icon list below)
- 3-4 key activities listed as small text (10pt), Left-aligned, with small navy bullet points
- Bottom metric strip: 1 success metric highlighted in Light Blue Tint (#E8F0FE)

Arrow connectors between phases:
- Solid navy right-arrow → between each block
- Between SUSTAIN and ASSESS: Curved arrow below the main row (dashed navy line, lighter opacity) with label "Continuous Iteration"

ABOVE the phase blocks (strip running full width):
- Label: "DIMENSIONS OF EACH PHASE" | Left-aligned header
- 4 colored horizontal bands running across all 5 phases:
  • People (emerald green, 18px stripe)
  • Process (amber/gold, 18px stripe)
  • Technology (Changeis Blue, 18px stripe)
  • Measurement (slate gray, 18px stripe)
- Each colored band runs as a continuous stripe across all 5 phases to show integrated nature

BELOW the phase blocks (strip running full width, ~100px):
- Three framework icons with labels: 
  • Prosci ADKAR (individual change foundation)
  • Kotter 8-Step (organizational momentum)
  • Bridges Transition Model (psychological safety)
  • NIST AI RMF (governance spine)
- Layout: 4 small outlined circular badges, centered, connected by small dotted line
- Label above: "Grounded In:" in small Slate Gray caps

Phase-Specific Icons (outline style, 48px):
- ASSESS: Magnifying glass over bar chart
- PLAN: Route/roadmap with waypoints
- PILOT: Flask/beaker with spark
- SCALE: Expanding arrows / growth diagram
- SUSTAIN: Circular arrow (cycle/refresh symbol)

Bottom Strip (40px):
- Changeis logo (left)
- Website URL: www.changeis.com (center)
- Tagline: "Aligning People, Processes and Technology" (right)
- Background: Deep Navy (#0D2B5E)

Overall Feel: Clean, structured, authoritative. Similar visual language to McKinsey or BCG framework diagrams — professional, credible, not cluttered.

PRODUCTION NOTES FOR DESIGNER

File Deliverables:
- All graphics in source format (Figma preferred, Adobe Illustrator acceptable)
- Export at 2× resolution for all digital assets (retina-ready)
- PDF export of all print/document-format graphics at 300 DPI
- Separate export of each social media variant at platform-optimal resolution

Icon Library:
- Source icons from Phosphor Icons, Heroicons, or Feather Icons (all free, consistent outline style)
- Do not mix icon libraries — consistency is critical for professional appearance

Accessibility:
- All text elements must meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast requirements
- Do not convey information through color alone (pair color coding with text labels or patterns)
- Provide alt-text recommendations for all final web-deployed graphics

Usage Priority Order (if design time is limited):
1. Graphic 1 (Framework Overview) — essential, highest impact
2. Graphic 3 (Adoption Gap Diagram) — campaign launch critical
3. Graphic 4 (Statistics Cards — batch of 3 minimum) — social cadence support
4. Graphics 2A-2E (Phase Cards series) — framework education series
5. Graphic 6 templates (Social Templates) — enables sustained content production
6. Graphics 5, 7, 8, 9 — secondary priority

Version Control:
- All graphics should be produced with a "versioned" naming convention (e.g., CAACMF_Framework_Overview_v1.0, CAACMF_StatCard_63pct_v1.0)
- Brand guidelines file should accompany all final deliverables
