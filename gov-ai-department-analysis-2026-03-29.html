Executive Summary
Key Insight for Changeis

The U.S. government filed 5,390 AI use cases in FY2025 — a 53.5% increase over FY2024. Nearly 3,000 are active deployments. The agencies growing fastest (NASA +2,261%, DOE +330%, Commerce +291%) are creating new procurement vehicles and vendor relationships right now. For Changeis's Innovation Lab: the DOE nuclear licensing AI breakthrough directly maps to FAA's ATLAS mission. For the FAA Oversight BAA: AI-assisted safety surveillance and documentation automation are the exact capabilities the FAA is seeking to procure in 2026.

DOE Nuclear → FAA ATLAS Connection

The DOE demonstrated AI converting a nuclear safety analysis document into NRC licensing documents in 1 day instead of weeks, using Microsoft Azure + Everstar's Gordian AI. This is architecturally identical to what FAA's ATLAS program needs: AI that can read legacy application documentation, identify gaps against FAA/NAS standards, and output compliant modernization recommendations. This is Changeis's Innovation Lab #1 priority.

FAA Oversight BAA — AI Application Opportunities

Varun referenced the "future of oversight FAA BAA" in this task. Here is the AI landscape most relevant to a potential FAA oversight/safety-focused BAA response:

  • AI-Augmented Safety Inspections: Computer vision + AI for automated inspection deficiency identification. DOE/NRC already validating this model for nuclear. FAA could adopt for aircraft maintenance facility inspections.
  • Regulatory Compliance Monitoring Agent: Continuous AI surveillance of regulated entity filings for compliance signals. Flags anomalies before violations occur. Reduces FAA inspector manual review burden.
  • Enforcement Decision Support: AI analyzes enforcement case history to recommend consistent enforcement actions. Reduces variance in inspector decision-making across regions.
  • FAA ODRA AI Integration: AI review of Office of Dispute Resolution case filings — similar to what Changeis already tracks via VM2. Could expand to predictive dispute risk scoring.
  • NAS Performance Analytics: AI analysis of NAS performance data to identify systemic risks before they manifest as delays or incidents. Predictive rather than reactive oversight.
