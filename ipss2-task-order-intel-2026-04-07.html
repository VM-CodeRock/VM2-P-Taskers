IPSS3 Strategic Implications for Changeis

Critical Observation: Active Task Orders Bridging Into IPSS3 Five IPSS2 task orders are still active as of April 2026 and extend well into the IPSS3 period — including the $15.3M ITS JPO EPMO (#693JJ320F000329) and $9.7M IT Governance (#693JJ321F000457). These represent direct transition opportunities: when they expire, DOT OAs will need replacement TORPs on IPSS3. Changeis must position now to capture these re-competes.
Pattern from IPSS2 IPSS3 Opportunity for Changeis Priority
IT Governance / Strategic Comms recurred annually for 8 consecutive years (~$155K–$1.3M per year) The OCIO will need this on IPSS3 from Day 1. Build a proposal narrative around DOT's April 2025 CIO consolidation memo. Target: first TORP win on this task area. IMMEDIATE
Cybersecurity grew from zero to 12% of total spend, with 5 separate task orders post-2019 FISMA/HVA support, Cyber EO implementation, Zero Trust — DOT OCIP is a new office that will need ongoing support. Changeis has NIST 800-53/171 credentials; build a DOT-specific cybersecurity narrative. HIGH
FHWA was the #2 OA by spend ($24.3M, 32% of total); ITS JPO EPMO alone was $15.3M FHWA OIT CPIC and capital planning (like IPSS2 #52) will likely be re-competed. Changeis's CLMRS capital investment planning experience maps directly. Pursue proactively. HIGH
NHTSA was the #3 OA ($8.1M). ODI Support ($6.2M) was a 6-year single award. NHTSA defects investigation program support and Data Act financial services are re-compete opportunities. No obvious Changeis incumbency, but vehicle access enables bidding. MEDIUM
PHMSA was awarded $7M in security and IT planning over 3 awards PHMSA security and IT strategic planning services will need a new home on IPSS3. These were sole-source-style awards to Avantus — Changeis can compete with cybersecurity credentials. MEDIUM
Application rationalization appeared 4× ($2.8M total) — a recurring OCIO need Application rationalization is now a standing OCIO capability need. Align with DOT's ongoing IT portfolio management and FITARA compliance obligations. MEDIUM
MARAD appeared late (FY2023) with EA and program visualization work — a new OA expanding onto vehicle Early-mover advantage on new OAs. MARAD, FRA, and FTA (not yet on IPSS2) are candidates to expand onto IPSS3. Changeis's FTA contract is the entry point. MEDIUM
Digital Transformation / Shared Services emerged 2018–2020 then faded — replaced by AI mandate DOT IT strategy now emphasizes AI, digital factories, and automation. Position the CLMRS Digital Factory as the proof case for what DOT wants across all OAs. STRATEGIC
Changeis Unique Angle: IPSS2 End-of-Life = IPSS3 First Mover Window The five currently-active IPSS2 task orders (total value: ~$25M) will all expire before Dec 2026. DOT OAs cannot bridge these onto IPSS2 indefinitely — they must be re-competed on IPSS3. Changeis is positioned to win at least 2–3 of these transitions if outreach to contracting officers and COR contacts begins within the next 60 days.
