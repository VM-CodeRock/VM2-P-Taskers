Technical Approach Evaluation (Rated)

What Evaluators Assess The likelihood of successful performance of the discriminating sections of the SOW. Evaluators are looking for HOW — not just what the offeror will do. Tools, methods, technologies, steps, deliverables, and specific actions.
RatingDefinitionWhat Changeis Needs to Achieve
Exceeds In addition to "Meets" requirements: cumulative strengths demonstrate HIGH likelihood of success OR identifies innovations/features that significantly benefit the project. Strengths significantly outweigh weaknesses. Target rating. Requires: specific innovations (e.g., AI-assisted correspondence tracking, proprietary compliance framework), demonstrated tools that go beyond SOW baseline, differentiating methodologies for Tasks 4 and 3.
Meets Responsive to factor requirements; demonstrates AVERAGE likelihood of success. Strengths don't support "Exceeds." Still likely to succeed despite weaknesses. Minimum competitive threshold. Not sufficient to win in an open competition. Avoids DQ but won't discriminate positively.
Unacceptable LOW likelihood of success. Weaknesses significantly outweigh strengths. Disqualification — no further evaluation. Changeis must avoid this at all costs.
