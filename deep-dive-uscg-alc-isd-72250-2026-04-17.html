Mission & Requirement

The USCG Aviation Logistics Center (ALC), located in Elizabeth City, NC, is the Coast Guard's primary depot-level maintenance, engineering, supply, and information management hub for its aviation fleet. Established in 1939 and officially named ALC in 1947, it supports all Coast Guard aircraft and serves 17,129+ users through its flagship system, the Aviation Logistics Information Management System (ALMIS).

The ALC Information Systems Division (ISD) is the ALC's internal IT department. Its primary mission is to deliver IT professional services supporting ALMIS, the Coast Guard Logistics Information Management System (CG-LIMS), and the organic Aviation Logistics Center Logistics Support System (ALC-LSS).

Required task areas include:

  • Applications development & web development
  • Database engineering & database administration
  • System engineering & business analysis
  • System security & information assurance
  • Procurement support
  • Help desk support & account management
  • Account auditing & configuration management
  • Quality assurance & project management
  • Asset management & network support
  • Configuration Control Board (CCB) facilitation
  • Process planning, data analysis & compliance (ISO/quality/safety standards)

The USCG has insufficient government civilian and military personnel to complete required tasks — contractor augmentation is mission-critical.

Risk Analysis
HIGH RISK
SDVOSB Certification Requirement
GSA VETS 2 is a strict SDVOSB-only GWAC. Changeis must hold SBA VetCert SDVOSB certification to prime. As of NDAA 2024, self-certification is no longer accepted — formal VetCert required. If not certified, Changeis must partner as a subcontractor or teammate with a certified SDVOSB prime. This is the single most important gate to assess immediately.
HIGH RISK
Oasys Incumbent Advantage
Oasys won the prior $120M award despite 13 competitors. Even with protests, their knowledge of USCG ALC systems, personnel, and processes is formidable. The bridge task order ($7.97M) kept Oasys on-contract through the recompete period, deepening their incumbent advantage. Changeis must build a compelling differentiation strategy — not just meet requirements but exceed them on key scoring criteria.
MEDIUM RISK
VETS 2 Contract Holder Required
Changeis must hold a GSA VETS 2 contract vehicle to prime. If not a current VETS 2 holder, a teaming arrangement with a VETS 2 prime is required. VETS 2 pool is large but relationship/teaming needs to be established well before solicitation close.
MEDIUM RISK
On-Site Presence in Elizabeth City, NC
ISD services require significant on-site presence at ALC Elizabeth City. This is a relatively remote location. Changeis must plan for local staffing or relocation packages to attract qualified IT professionals to this site. Travel costs and staffing pipeline must be factored into pricing.
MEDIUM RISK
Security Clearance Requirements
ALMIS and CG-LIMS handle operational logistics for all Coast Guard assets. Likely requires at minimum Secret clearances for some staff, with possible Sensitive Compartmented Information (SCI) requirements for certain modules. Clearance timelines and costs must be incorporated into proposal strategy.
LOW RISK
Timeline Compression
Solicitation was estimated for March 30, 2026 — this may already be released. Award targeted July 10, 2026. Short response window likely. Team must be assembled and capture activities completed now to be competitive at solicitation release.
