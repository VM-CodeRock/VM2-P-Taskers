Project Summary — MKT.AIC

The Aviation Innovation Coalition (AIC) is a newly formed industry group of Founding Members — including Changeis / Varun Malhotra — convened by Crossroads Strategies to advance FAA Air Traffic Control modernization through coordinated industry advocacy.

The Coalition successfully completed Phase 1 Hill and DOT outreach on April 8, 2026, conducting 6 meetings with House and Senate appropriators, authorizers, and DOT's Under Secretary for Policy. All meetings were supportive. DOT Under Secretary McCormack provided key referrals to Secretary Duffy's Chief of Staff and the Deputy Secretary.

Current focus: Board structure finalization, press release, website launch, membership dues collection, and scheduling Phase 2 follow-on meetings with FAA Administrator Bedford and DOT senior leadership. Three action items are open for Varun: Board rep photo/bio submission, invoicing contact, and preparation of Changeis ATC modernization materials for Phase 2 meetings.

Strategic significance: AIC provides Changeis a formal seat at the table in FAA ATC modernization policy — directly aligned with Changeis's FAA portfolio (APEX, ATLAS, etc.). Founding Member status and a Board seat position Varun as an influential voice shaping how $12.5B in FAA modernization funding is deployed.
