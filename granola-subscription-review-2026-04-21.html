What Granola Does

Granola is an AI-powered meeting notepad that captures audio directly from your Mac or Windows device — no bot joins your call. Unlike Fireflies, Otter, and Fathom, which send a visible recording participant into meetings, Granola runs silently as a system-level sidecar.

How it works

Bot-Free Transcription

  • Captures system audio + microphone locally on-device
  • No "Granola Bot has joined" notification in meetings
  • Works with any video platform (Zoom, Teams, Meet, WebEx) without integration
  • Live transcript viewable during the meeting
  • Auto-detects meetings via calendar sync
Human-in-the-loop notes

AI-Enhanced Note Taking

  • You type your own notes during the meeting — your emphasis, your priorities
  • Granola enhances them post-meeting using the full transcript
  • Your notes appear in black; AI additions in gray — visually distinct
  • AI chat lets you ask questions across meetings
  • Available on macOS, Windows, and iOS
Key differentiator: Granola's human-in-the-loop approach produces notes shaped by what you found important, not a generic AI summary. Particularly useful for exec meetings where sensitive topics make a visible bot awkward or inappropriate.
