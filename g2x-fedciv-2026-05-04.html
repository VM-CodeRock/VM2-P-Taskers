Week of May 4 Macro Context: The Trump administration's FY2027 budget proposes $75.7B in civilian IT—up from $67.9B in FY2026—signaling a recovery in procurement volume after DOGE-driven cancellations. GSA's procurement consolidation push is expected to route ~50% of all federal procurement through GSA-managed vehicles by year-end, reshaping task order competition across MAS, OASIS+, and SEWP. The DHS Cumulus cloud mega-contract and FAA ATLAS IT modernization challenge are the two dominant near-term vehicles shaping FedCiv BD strategy.

Key Findings This Week

  • ›DHS Cumulus Cloud (Block 1): DHS has begun staggered direct awards to AWS, Microsoft, Google Cloud, and Oracle for its centralized cloud vehicle. AWS award expected by end of June; remaining three by end of Q3 FY26. Block 2 multi-award IDIQ opens competitive sub-contracting lanes.
  • ›FAA ATLAS IT Modernization: Challenge-based acquisition for ~200 apps and 3,000 databases. Phase 4 (award) targeted Sept. 30, 2026 for a 10-year contract. Peraton already named prime integrator for ATC modernization; ATLAS is a separate enterprise IT portfolio effort.
  • ›USDA Palantir $300M BPA + Steampunk $270M IMDI: Two massive USDA awards this cycle confirm the department is one of the most active buyers. First Palantir task order under the NFSAP BPA announced April 22—expect follow-on task orders through FY27.
  • ›DHS Databricks & Data Fabric BPAs: DHS HQ/MGMT issued two separate forecasts (F2026073326) for enterprise data analytics platforms, with solicitation released April 24 and award expected Q3 FY26—a near-term opportunity for analytics/ML service providers.
  • ›FinCEN Regulatory Writing Sources Sought: Treasury's Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) released SS-FIN-26-032 on April 29 with a May 13 close date—a small-business-accessible near-term opportunity for GovTech consulting firms.

Detailed Opportunity Profiles

1. DHS Cumulus Enterprise Cloud (Block 1 + Block 2)
$100M+
DHS HQ NAICS 518210 IDIQ / Direct Award Award Imminent
Description: DHS's centralized cloud computing vehicle consolidating commercial cloud procurement across all DHS components. Block 1 consists of non-competitive direct contracts with AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. Block 2 is a forthcoming competitive multiple-award IDIQ creating subcontracting and teaming opportunities for service providers.

BD Angle: Direct hyperscaler awards are sole-source, but Block 2 competitive IDIQ opens managed services, integration, and migration task orders. Track subcontracting opportunities with each hyperscaler's federal divisions. DHS's 11.7B FY2027 IT budget is the second-largest civilian allocation.
Next Milestone: AWS direct award expected by end of June 2026 (Q2 FY26) · MSFT, GCP, OCI awards expected by end of September 2026 (Q3 FY26) · One-year base + four option years each
2. FAA ATLAS — Accelerated Transformation of Legacy Applications & Systems
TBD (10-yr)
DOT / FAA NAICS 541511 FAA AMS Challenge Phase 3 Eval
Description: FAA's multi-phase challenge-based acquisition to rationalize, consolidate, and cloud-migrate 200+ applications and ~3,000 databases across the mission-support portfolio. Phase 1 required corporate experience documentation (due March 10); Phase 2 evaluated portfolio rationalization strategy; Phase 3 required vendors to demonstrate a live "modernization factory." Phase 4 (award) targeted September 30, 2026.

BD Angle: Top 10 Phase 1 scorers advanced to Phase 2; only 5 finalists progress to Phase 3 demo. Self-scoring matrix rewards $25M, $50M, and $100M+ annual contract experience, AI deployments, and cloud migration track record. Separate from the Peraton ATC Prime Integrator role. 10-year performance period; value could exceed $1B based on scope.
Next Milestone: Phase 4 RFP issuance and contract award targeted September 30, 2026 · 10-year period of performance
3. USDA / Palantir National Farm Security Action Plan BPA — Follow-on Task Orders
$300M BPA
USDA OCIO NAICS 541512 BPA (Sole Source) Ongoing TOs
Description: USDA and Palantir Technologies formalized a $300M Blanket Purchase Agreement on April 22, 2026 supporting the National Farm Security Action Plan (NFSAP) and the "One Farmer, One File" data unification initiative. The five-year BPA delivers advanced operational software to government field personnel. First task order announced April 22, formalizing farm security data analytics.

BD Angle: Sole-source BPA limits direct competition, but generates downstream integration, training, and change management task order opportunities. Monitor USDA OCIO procurement forecast for companion requirements. Palantir's concurrent Treasury API/data contract signals a cross-agency AI analytics pattern.
Next Milestone: Follow-on TOs expected throughout FY26–FY27 · Watch SAM.gov under USDA OCIO for companion solicitations

~$100M est.

BD Angle: Competitive BPA with multiple award lanes. Firm-fixed-price. DHS Acquisition Planning Forecast System Record F2026073326. Solicitation released April 24. Requires Databricks platform experience and federal cloud security posture.
Next Milestone: Solicitation released April 24, 2026 · Award Q3 FY2026 · Contract runs through 2031
