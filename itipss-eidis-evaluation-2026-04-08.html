2. Technical Scope — EIDIS / EIDIP Phase 1

Based on the email from OST Global and FAA's known Enterprise Information Management (EIM) Platform, EIDIS encompasses the FAA's enterprise data integration modernization program. The FAA's AIT office operates the EIM Platform to collect, store, curate, enrich, access, and analyze FAA data across all programs.

Program Background — FAA EIM Platform
The FAA's AIT office manages approximately 200 applications and 3,000 databases across the agency. The EIM Platform serves as the shared enterprise data infrastructure enabling programs to avoid duplicative data pipelines. EIDIS is the modernization initiative to build Phase 1 of an enterprise-scale data integration platform.
