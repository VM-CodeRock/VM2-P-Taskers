AJI-3 Background & Mission

The Policy and Performance Directorate (AJI-3) supports the ATO Vice President of Safety and is responsible for managing the ATO's Safety Management System (SMS). AJI-3 is the nerve center for FAA aviation safety governance, encompassing:

  • Managing ATO SMS — policies, documentation, facilitation, training for 14,000+ air traffic controllers
  • Conducting Independent Operational Assessments (IOAs) to determine operational readiness for NAS system deployments
  • Auditing and assessing SMS quality across FAA facilities
  • Analyzing leading safety indicators, root causes of incidents, and maintaining safety metrics
  • Supporting international safety outreach via CANSO, ICAO, and other organizations
  • Managing In-Service Decision (ISD) acquisition milestones
