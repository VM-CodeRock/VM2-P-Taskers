Current Intelligence Landscape (March 2026)

Several forces are reshaping federal IT contracting as of this review date. These directly inform topic selection:

  • DOGE sunset approaching (July 4, 2026): Over 10,000 contracts terminated since January 2025. IT modernization spend remains strong — nearly $50B/quarter — but agencies require cost-benefit justification for all IT advisory and consulting work. Changeis must demonstrate quantifiable outcomes.
  • GSA GSAR AI Clause (GSAR 552.239-7001): Proposed March 6, 2026; public comment closes April 3. Mandates "American AI Systems," 72-hour incident reporting, government data ownership, and flowdown liability to subcontractors. Directly affects Changeis AI/ML delivery approach.

  • DHS surveillance tech surge: DHS received $191B+ from "One Big Beautiful Bill." Palantir BPA at $1B ceiling. $50M+ mobile surveillance, $100M+ modular surveillance systems in pipeline. CISA has 12+ recompetes expected in 2026.
  • Federal AI policy unification: Trump EO on unified AI framework drives agencies toward single AI standards. Agentic AI moving from pilot to production across civilian agencies.
