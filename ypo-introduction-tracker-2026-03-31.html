Process Guide — Varun's Membership Committee Role

As Vice Chair of the YPO DC Membership Committee, Varun Malhotra is responsible for managing the front end of the member pipeline — from initial introduction through the first substantive conversation. This guide provides quick reference for the full process.

YPO DC Membership Eligibility — Quick Reference
  • Age: Must be under 45 years old at time of application
  • Role: Must be a top executive (CEO, President, Managing Director, owner/founder) with ultimate operational or ownership responsibility
  • Scale: Company must meet YPO revenue or headcount thresholds (varies by category — confirm current requirements with the chapter)
  • Character: Strong reputation, no significant ethical concerns, genuine interest in peer learning
Varun's Responsibilities
  • Respond to new introductions within 24–48 hours
  • Task Donna Shea to schedule 1:1 within 2 weeks of introduction
  • Conduct the 1:1 meeting — exploratory, warm, not a formal interview
  • Document outcome and next steps in this tracker
  • Circle back to introducer (Eliana or other) within one week post-meeting
  • Guide strong candidates toward the application process
  • Handle graceful declines when a candidate is not a fit
After the 1:1 Meeting — Decision Framework
