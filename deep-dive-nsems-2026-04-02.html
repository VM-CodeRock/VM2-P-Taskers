The Facilities and Engineering Services (AJW-2) directorate — part of the Air Traffic Organization's Technical Operations service unit — is responsible for the safe and effective lifecycle management of the NAS facilities infrastructure. Its mission is to manage and execute programs and projects to safely establish, replace, modernize, and sustain the NAS.

NSEMS seeks contractor support across three mission pillars encompassing 13 Functional Work Areas and 8 Program Management Work Areas. The scope requires a broad range of labor families, resource categories, and skill levels to support existing NAS operations as well as planning and integration of new entrants (UAS, NextGen technologies).
