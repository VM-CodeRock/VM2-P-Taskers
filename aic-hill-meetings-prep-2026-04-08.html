ALERT Act vs. ROTOR Act — The Key Tension
  • House ALERT Act (passed T&I 62-0, March 26): Requires collision avoidance tech by Dec 2031; allows any technology including portable ADS-B. Senate Commerce says it's too weak.
  • Senate ROTOR Act: Passed Senate unanimously Dec 2025. Mandates ADS-B In specifically — the NTSB's explicit 18-time recommendation. Fell 2 votes short of 2/3 threshold in House.
  • Cruz/Cantwell statement (March 26): Any bill passing both chambers "will have to apply the strongest ADS-B In safety standards to all aircraft, civil and military."
  • Reconciliation path: House floor vote on ALERT Act is next. Senate will likely demand conference or amendment to strengthen ADS-B In requirement.
