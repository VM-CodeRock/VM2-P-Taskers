India's DJ Scene — Tracks & Apple Playlist

14 DJs from Warpaint Journal's Instagram Feature · Curated Playlist + Similar Artists
Created: 2026-04-15  ·  Source: @warpaintjournal on Instagram
About this playlist: Warpaint Journal's April 2026 Instagram carousel featured 14 DJs who collectively represent India's DJ scene — from Sunburn festival headliners to underground techno selectors, folk-house innovators to Bollywood remix specialists. This guide covers their signature tracks, style profiles, Apple Music links, and a full curated playlist order. Slide 1 was the intro card; DJs appear in slides 2–17.
The 14 DJs — Profiles & Top Tracks
Nucleya
Slide 2
Desi Bass Indian Electronic Folk Fusion
Udyan Sagar is the undisputed pioneer of "desi-bass" — blending Indian folk vocals, street sounds, and face-melting bass drops into a wholly unique sonic identity. His live shows are theatrical spectacles; he debuted his album Bass Rani on a truck at Mumbai's Ganesh festival.
  • 1
    Laung Gawacha Signature desi-bass anthem — Punjabi folk vocal over thunderous low-end. The track that introduced millions to his sound. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Bass Rani (feat. Divine, Shruti Pathak) Title track of his breakthrough 2015 album — explosive collaboration with rapper Divine, remains his most-streamed release. ↗ Apple Music
  • 3
    Akkad Bakkad From his Koocha Monster EP — frantic, multi-layered, showcases his sample-chopping genius across Tamil, Bengali, and Punjabi. ↗ Apple Music
  • 4
    Scene Kya Hai (feat. Divine) From Raja Baja (2016) — gritty Mumbai hip-hop meets woozy bass wobble; represents the desi-bass + rap intersection. ↗ Apple Music
Lost Stories
Slide 3
Progressive House Folk Fusion Trance
Duo Prayag Mehta & Rishab Joshi, ranked #52 in DJ Mag's Top 100 (2016-17) — the first Indians to play Tomorrowland four times. They blend Indian folk with progressive electronic textures. Also produce under the alias PUNA for pop artists.
  • 1
    Bombay Dreams (with KSHMR, feat. Kavita Seth) Their biggest international collaboration — sitar-tinged progressive house with classical folk vocals. Released on KSHMR's Dharma Worldwide label. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Mahi (feat. Kavita Seth) Premiered in 2015, finally released 2019 — five years of fan anticipation paid off. Luminous folk vocals over euphoric progressive build. ↗ Apple Music
  • 3
    Faded (Alan Walker Remix) Their Indian/tropical remix of the global smash — went viral during Walker's India tour; one of the most-shared Indian remix moments. ↗ Apple Music
  • 4
    Mai Ni Meriye (feat. Jonita Gandhi) Warm Punjabi folk meets polished electronic production — exemplifies their signature "folk-electronic" bridge sound. ↗ Apple Music
Arjun Vagale
Slide 4
Techno Minimal Underground
India's premier techno export. Atmospheric, intense, and technically surgical — his sets are hypnotic journeys rooted in the Berlin/Ibiza underground tradition. Runs Semantica Records releases and is co-owner of UnMute Agency. Currently reigns atop DJ Mag India's poll.
  • 1
    Terrakoz His most-celebrated production — a slow-burning, hypnotic techno epic praised by virtually every major DJ in the genre. Released on Trapez. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Spektron From his Beatport Top 10 — dense, industrial-influenced techno with complex rhythmic layering. Blueprint of his production style. ↗ Apple Music
  • 3
    Before the After On Semantica Records — moody, rolling techno that exemplifies his "intense, driven, atmospheric" trademark style. ↗ Apple Music
  • 4
    Rambutan Hard, percussive techno release on Semantica — shows his command of dancefloor tension and release. ↗ Apple Music
AFTERALL
Slide 5
Bolly Tech Tech House Afro House
A visual-first DJ whose sets are immersive experiences — live edits, custom lighting, and crowd interaction elevate Bollywood nostalgia into high-production club moments. He operates between Bollywood, tech house, afro house, and Latin influences, making his sets wildly genre-fluid.
  • 1
    TADKE TADKE (Original Mix) His most-shared original production — a bold bollytech banger that circulates widely in desi DJ circles. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 2
    Ole Ole Mashup (2026) His Afro House rework of the 90s Bollywood classic — tribal rhythm meets Bollywood nostalgia, recently viral. ↗ Instagram Reel
  • 3
    Jhumka X Aye Papi X Bebot (AFTERAll Edit) A genre-blending edit that went viral — captures his core ability to connect Bollywood hooks with global club sounds. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 4
    Paranda (AFTERAll Edit) Afro/tech rework of the Bollywood classic — demonstrates his Tomorrowland-circuit sound with Indian sensibility. ↗ SoundCloud
DJ Lemon
Slide 6
Bollywood Remix Commercial Progressive
A college-festival and club circuit stalwart whose Bollywood remixes have garnered hundreds of millions of YouTube views. His commercial sets are accessible but crafted — crowd interaction is as central to his performance as the music selection itself.
  • 1
    Woh Lamhe (DJ Lemon × Shaikh Brothers Remix) 30M+ YouTube views — his most-streamed remix; transformed Atif Aslam's heartbreak ballad into an anthemic remix. ↗ YouTube Music
  • 2
    Teri Meri (Lemon Mix) His early signature remix — smooth, melodic Bollywood-meets-progressive that defined his commercial sound. ↗ YouTube Music
  • 3
    Manjha Remix Festival-ready Bollywood remix showcasing his ability to build crowd energy through familiar melodies. ↗ YouTube Music
  • 4
    Daru Badnaam Remix One of his biggest festival remixes — infectious Punjabi party anthem elevated into a club-friendly format. ↗ YouTube Music
Sartek
Slide 7
Folk House Afro House Bolly Tech
One of India's most internationally aligned DJs — performed alongside AR Rahman, Justin Bieber, Armin van Buuren, and Hardwell. His folk-house sound blends Rajasthani and Sufi traditions with contemporary house production. Has releases on Sony Music, Armada, Revealed, and Spinnin.
  • 1
    Taal Tech (Extended Mix) His most-played original — 27K+ SoundCloud plays; traditional Indian taal rhythms woven into a modern house groove. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Ghum Shum (Extended Mix) Sufi-influenced folk house with haunting vocal textures — one of his most-discussed emotional productions. ↗ Apple Music
  • 3
    Mehendi (Folk House / Afro Indian) Recent release combining mehndi ceremony music tradition with Afro house percussion — nostalgic yet futuristic. ↗ Apple Music
  • 4
    Sufi Tech Released 2025 — his most sophisticated folk-house offering, drawing international praise on the ADE circuit. ↗ Apple Music
Karan Kanchan
Slide 8
Trap Hip-Hop Production Bass
Multi-genre producer and DJ based in Mumbai — co-founder of Neck Wreck Crew, managed by Gully Gang. Has produced for DIVINE, Pusha-T, Russ, and KING. His DJ sets fuse trap, bass, and Indian hip-hop in an intentional, tension-building way that avoids predictable drops.
  • 1
    Black Belt (feat. VinDon) 40K+ YouTube views — his most-recognized artist release; demonstrates his J-Trap / dark Indian hip-hop production style. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Daruma Dub Instrumental production showcase — 23K views; dub-influenced bass design that shows his range beyond rap production. ↗ Spotify
  • 3
    DIVINE production beats (various) His collaborative productions with DIVINE and the Gully Gang have shaped modern desi rap's sonic identity. ↗ Spotify Trap & Bass India
DJ Pearl
Slide 11
Techno Club Underground
A founding figure of India's electronic scene — co-founder of Submerge, one of the country's earliest club culture infrastructure initiatives. Her sets reflect two decades of experience and consistency, making her one of the most respected names in Indian DJing with deep underground credibility.
  • 1
    Been Done It to Em (Pearl Flip) Club tool edit that circulates in underground DJ circles — raw, percussive, and built for peak-hour pressure. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 2
    Kome On (Pearl Flip) — Club Tool Industrial-edged club edit that showcases her peak-hour DJing sensibility and technical precision. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 3
    Pavé An original production — minimal, atmospheric, deeply club-focused. Represents her mature production voice. ↗ SoundCloud
DJ Shaan
Slide 12
Festival EDM Commercial Progressive
A 28-year-old Sunburn mainstage artist with a Republic Records deal — his original tracks and remixes, including the charting "Light Up the World," have earned him significant international platform exposure. Represents India's commercial EDM presence on the global festival circuit.
  • 1
    Light Up the World His internationally charting original — big-room progressive house that earned him a Republic Records deal and global playlist placement. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 2
    Paradise (feat. SAKIMA) 108K+ SoundCloud plays — melodic progressive house with emotive vocal production; one of his most-loved originals. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 3
    By Heart 38K+ plays — warm, emotional progressive release that shows his range beyond festival EDM bombast. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 4
    Nirma (DJ Shaan x Endrate House Edit) His recent foray into house — shows maturing production sensibility beyond the Sunburn mainstage formula. ↗ SoundCloud
Ankytrixx
Slide 13
Techno Deep Techno Underground
Called "India's #1 techno artist" — Ankytrixx has been building India's underground dance culture since his early career, playing globally while remaining deeply rooted in the club circuit. His Mixmag Lab Mumbai set (recorded at antiSocial) remains a benchmark of Indian techno DJing.
  • 1
    Mixmag Lab Mumbai Set (antiSocial) His defining recorded performance — a deep, layered techno set that showcased the maturity of India's underground scene to a global audience. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 2
    DGTL India Boiler Room Set His Boiler Room appearance at DGTL India — high-pressure, club-focused techno that placed him on the international radar. ↗ SoundCloud
  • 3
    Selected original productions Prolific producer across the decade — his SoundCloud catalog spans dark techno to progressive, each demonstrating production depth. ↗ SoundCloud
Sickflip
Slide 14
Alternative Electronic World Music Bass
Sarvesh Srivastava, known as Sickflip, has been shaping India's alternative electronic space since 1997 — over 30 years in the scene. His sets blend World, Hip-Hop, and bass textures in an organic, unpredictable way. Appeared at DGTL India and has built a distinct sonic identity that resists easy categorisation.
  • 1
    DGTL India Live Set His Boiler Room-broadcast performance at DGTL India — a defining display of his world/bass/hip-hop hybrid approach. ↗ SoundCloud / Boiler Room
  • 2
    Selected mixtapes and studio productions His mixtape catalog is where his unique vision lives — heavy synth, bass architecture, and global sound design textures. ↗ SoundCloud
Praveen Achary
Slide 15
Progressive House Deep Techno Melodic
Runs Juicebox Music — India's premier melodic/deep progressive house imprint. Regularly charted on Beatport's Top 100 Progressive House charts. Has officially remixed Above & Beyond, Cid Inc, and Eelke Kleijn. He has shared decks with John Digweed and Richie Hawtin, anchoring him in the global progressive techno circuit.
  • 1
    Circuit Valley (Praveen Achary Remix) His Beatport #1 remix — fluid progressive house architecture demonstrating his command of melodic tension and release. ↗ Spotify / Apple Music
  • 2
    Tejomaya (Praveen Achary Remix) Beatport Top 10 remix — hypnotic, meditative progressive house; the title means "radiant" in Sanskrit, reflecting its luminous quality. ↗ Spotify
  • 3
    Fiasco His 2021 single on Juicebox Music — deep, atmospheric progressive that shows his production identity stripped of remix constraints. ↗ Spotify
Ash Roy
Slide 16
Techno Deep House Soupherb Records
Founder of Soupherb Records — a label he built as a respected space for forward-thinking electronic music. His sound sits at the intersection of deep, organic percussion and driving techno. Trained in Germany, his music carries a refined, boundary-led sensibility that consistently receives critical acclaim on Beatport.
  • 1
    Plasticine Palace (Original Mix) His signature Soupherb Records track — textured, deep techno with organic percussion design. His Beatport #2 top track. ↗ Beatport / Apple Music
  • 2
    Early Beach (Ash Roy Remix) His #1 Beatport track — a remix that exemplifies his ability to inject driving groove into melodic material. ↗ Beatport
  • 3
    Fantastic Futures (Original Mix) On Soupherb Records — propulsive, forward-thinking techno that captures the label's ethos: refined, boundary-led. ↗ Beatport
Kohra
Slide 17
Techno Qilla Records Global
A key architect of Indian techno — his label Qilla Records (est. 2011) is the most important Indian techno imprint. Has shared line-ups with Pete Tong and Richie Hawtin, performed at ADE, and consistently delivers sets of precision, reach, and intent. His music carries Indian sonic heritage subtly and powerfully.
  • 1
    Tariq His most-recommended production on Reddit's techno communities — deep, Indian-textured techno that earns repeated mentions as an entry point to his work. ↗ Apple Music
  • 2
    Jatayu Named after the mythological vulture-king — heavy, ritual techno with a mythic atmosphere. On Fur:ther Sessions; Beatport Top 2 track. ↗ Apple Music
  • 3
    A Bird Above the Clouds Qilla Records release — expansive, cinematic techno that shows his full range as a sonic storyteller. ↗ Apple Music
  • 4
    Acid Kidz On Qilla Records — a rare acid-meets-Indian-techno fusion that demonstrates his fearless genre experimentation. ↗ Apple Music

How to Create This Playlist in Apple Music

Apple Music does not have a public API for automated playlist creation. Here are the two best methods to build this playlist:

Method 1 — Manual (Fastest, 5 minutes)

1
Open Apple Music on iPhone, Mac, or iPad. Click File → New Playlist (Mac) or the + button in the Library tab (iPhone).
2
Name it: "India DJ Scene — Warpaint Journal" or any name you prefer.
3
Use the search bar to find each track above. Click the ⋯ → Add to Playlist option. Work through the playlist table top-to-bottom for the curated order.
4
For tracks not on Apple Music (AFTERALL, DJ Pearl originals), add the SoundCloud link to Apple Music's "station" or listen separately — or substitute with similar tracks.

Method 2 — Apple Shortcuts Automation

1
Open the Shortcuts app on iPhone/Mac. Create a new Shortcut and add the action Music → Create Playlist.
2
Add individual Music → Add to Playlist actions for each track, specifying the playlist name created in the previous step.
3
Run the Shortcut — it will create the playlist and add all available tracks automatically.
