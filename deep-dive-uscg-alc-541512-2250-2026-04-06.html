Requirement Analysis

The USCG Aviation Logistics Center (ALC) in Elizabeth City, NC is the Coast Guard's primary aviation depot — providing maintenance, engineering, supply, and acquisition support for the service's entire aviation fleet. The ALC Information Systems Division (ISD) functions as the IT department for this installation, supporting three enterprise logistics systems:

  • ALMIS — Aviation Logistics Management Information System (aviation asset tracking)
  • CG-LIMS — Coast Guard Logistics Information Management System (enterprise logistics)
  • ALC-LSS — Aviation Logistics Center Logistics Support System (organic ALC systems)
