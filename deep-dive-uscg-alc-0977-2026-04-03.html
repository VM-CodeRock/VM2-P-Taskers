2) Requirement Summary (APFS)

The USCG requires Training and Development Specialists to support engineering processes, quality assurance data reviews, technical and training support for the USCG and Department of Homeland Security (DHS). The Contractor shall sustain current and future users of ALMIS and/or future software enterprise system replacements. The Contractor shall support the USCG with trained and experienced personnel fully proficient in the trades of engineering services, configuration management, instructing and analysis.

The required services generally include, but are not limited to:

• Provide ALMIS/CG-LIMS functionality training to both USCG and non-USCG customers.
• Provide Maintenance Policy and User Publication Reviews.
• Provide Field Unit Support.
• Provide data review and analysis of ALMIS data.
