Evaluation Criteria

The platform scores each applicant 1–10 (lowest to highest) across five categories. Each dimension carries equal weight.

1Academic Performance
Overall academic success, GPA, rigor of coursework, and demonstrated motivation to achieve.
2Leadership & Character
Teamwork, history of achievement, attitude, leadership roles, community involvement, and extracurriculars.
3Community Service
Volunteer work, positive influence on community, and service beyond self-interest.
4Financial Need
Financial circumstances and demonstrated barriers. FAFSA SAI, parent income, and student savings considered.
5Aviation / Aerospace Career Commitment
Passion for aviation/aerospace, clarity of career goals, alignment with the scholarship's mission, and concrete steps taken (licenses, training, internships, research).
Scoring Note Financial need scoring is calibrated: SAI 0 = 10/10; SAI <5,000 = 9/10; SAI 5k–15k = 7–8; SAI 15k–40k = 5–6; SAI >40k or no FAFSA but high parent income = 3–4; very high parent income (≥$180k) with no FAFSA = 2. No FAFSA + unclear circumstances = 5. Aviation commitment is weighted by actual steps taken (certificates, internships, research) not just stated interest.
