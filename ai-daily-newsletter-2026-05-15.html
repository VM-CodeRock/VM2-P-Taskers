Google · Gemini 3.1 Ultra

Gemini 3.1 Ultra Ships with 2-Million Token Native Context Window — Text, Image, Audio, and Video Simultaneously

Google launched Gemini 3.1 Ultra in early May 2026, its most capable model to date. The headline specification is a native 2-million token context window that operates across all modalities simultaneously — text, image, audio, and video — without transcription intermediaries. In practical terms, 2M tokens is equivalent to 6–8 hours of audio, 4–5 hours of video, or the entire multi-year customer interaction history of an enterprise client. This is the model now powering GenAI.mil Agent Designer at the Pentagon, referenced in prior reporting, and serves as the backbone for tomorrow's Google I/O showcase. For federal contractors, Gemini 3.1 Ultra's multimodal long context creates viable use cases in document analysis, multi-source synthesis, and regulatory review at volumes previously impractical.
