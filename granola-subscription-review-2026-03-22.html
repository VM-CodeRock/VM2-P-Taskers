2) How to cancel or downgrade (self-serve)

Granola’s documentation describes a Stripe billing portal flow:

  • Granola app → Settings → Billing → Manage subscription (opens Stripe portal)
  • Stripe portal → Cancel subscription (or change plan)

What happens after you cancel: your subscription continues until the end of the current billing period, then you move to the free (Basic) plan.

Potential gotcha: on the free plan, Granola only shows notes from the last 30 days in-app (older notes are stored but not accessible in the app).

3) Decision checklist (quick)

Cancel if…

  • You aren’t using Granola weekly for meeting capture.
  • You don’t need long-term in-app access/search beyond 30 days.
  • You aren’t using Business-only capabilities (advanced models and integrations).

Keep if…

  • Granola is core to your meeting workflow and you rely on long-term history.
  • You actively use (or plan to use) integrations (e.g., Notion/Slack/CRM).
  • You want unlimited note history available in-app.

4) Suggested next action (5 minutes)

  1. Open Granola → Settings → Billing → Manage subscription.
  2. In the Stripe portal, confirm: plan tier, next renewal date, seat count (if workspace), and payment method.
  3. If cancelling: optionally export what you need first, then choose Cancel subscription in Stripe.
