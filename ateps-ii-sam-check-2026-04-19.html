5 · Context & Strategic Notes

Changeis is a confirmed ATEPS-II IDIQ holder. This positions the firm to compete for task orders issued by FAA's Air Traffic Organization (ATO) across four directorates: Integrated Services & Analysis (AJM-1), Air Traffic Systems (AJM-2), Enterprise Services (AJM-3), and Surveillance Services (AJM-4). The 10-year vehicle runs through approximately 2034.

What this vehicle covers: ATEPS-II funds program management, systems engineering, software development, field engineering, system implementation, standards, rulemaking support, and NextGen initiative support across the ATO. This closely parallels Changeis's concurrent ATAPS-II award ($103M, July 2024) — giving the firm complementary IDIQ coverage across ATO program support.

FAA ODRA protest history: Changeis has a prior FAA ODRA case history related to ATEPS-era procurements. The ODRA was also the venue for the 2022 Noblis suspension request (Case D22-00915) following ATEPS-I awards. Noblis ultimately won an unrestricted seat on ATEPS-II (Dec 2024). No open ODRA cases related to ATEPS-II have been identified as of this check date.

Adjacent FAA opportunity to watch: The FAA's PMO Technical Assistance Contract (PTAC), a $1.3B recompete in the same ATO program management space, had an RFP expected December 2025 with award estimated May 2026. This is a parallel opportunity relevant to Changeis's ATO portfolio.
