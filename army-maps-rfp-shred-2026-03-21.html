Prepared: 2026-03-21 • Focus: CMMC Level 2 feasibility + matrix template

Executive takeaways (CMMC L2 “fact test”)

  • If MAPS requires an active CMMC Level 2 certificate at proposal submission: you likely cannot rely on an “in-progress” posture; you’d need the certificate or an explicit solicitation allowance for conditional compliance.
  • If CMMC is scored but not pass/fail: ISO 27001 + a well-documented CMMC program can still be competitive, but you’ll likely lose points versus certified bidders.
  • ISO 27001 is strong evidence of mature governance and maps well to many controls, but it is not a substitute for CMMC L2 if a certificate is mandated.
  • Facility clearance (Secret/TS) often appears as gate criteria or scoring credit on large Army vehicles; position clearance evidence and operational benefits clearly.
