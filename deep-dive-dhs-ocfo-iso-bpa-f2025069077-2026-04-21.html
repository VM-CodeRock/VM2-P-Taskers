Scope Breakdown — What This BPA Covers:

  • ISO QMS Maintenance: Maintain and continuously improve DHS OCFO's ISO 9001:2015-certified Quality Management System — documentation, internal audits, corrective actions, and surveillance audit support.
  • ISO QMS SharePoint Maintenance & Enhancement: Build and maintain SharePoint sites hosting QMS documentation, process flows, and controlled records accessible to OCFO staff.
  • ISO Training: Design and deliver ISO 9001 awareness training, internal auditor training, and process owner coaching across OCFO divisions.
  • ISO Strategic Guidance: Advise OCFO leadership on quality culture maturation, QMS expansion to additional OCFO divisions, and alignment with OMB/GAO audit standards.
  • Process Optimization & Business Process Improvement (BPI): Analyze, redesign, and document financial management and operational processes to eliminate inefficiencies and support clean audit opinion.
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA) Development & Governance: Develop bots for repetitive financial reporting and data reconciliation tasks; establish RPA governance framework and bot lifecycle management.
  • Organization Restructure Support, Strategic Planning, Workforce Planning & Modeling: Advisory support for OCFO organizational design, workforce capacity analysis, and strategic roadmaps.
  • OFO Customer Engagement Activities, Workforce Training and Development, Technical Advice: Customer-facing engagement for OCFO's Office of Financial Operations (OFO) programs.

DHS Clean Audit Opinion Context: DHS achieved its first-ever clean financial statement audit opinion in recent fiscal years after decades of disclaimers. OCFO's ISO QMS program is a core pillar of that achievement — standardized, documented, and auditable processes are what auditors require to issue a clean opinion. This BPA is not discretionary: it directly funds the infrastructure keeping DHS audit-ready. Mission criticality is high; budget risk to this vehicle is low.

BPA Structure — Multiple Award Means Multiple Winners: Unlike a single-award IDIQ, a Multiple-Award BPA means the government intends to establish relationships with several qualified vendors. Each awardee is then eligible for individual BPA call orders competed among BPA holders. This structure (1) lowers the individual competition threshold versus a single-award, and (2) allows Changeis to win a BPA slot without displacing Guidehouse entirely — the two can coexist on the vehicle.
