Junior Cloud Engineer Junior · 0–3 Yrs
Position Overview

Changeis, Inc. is seeking a Junior Cloud Engineer to support enterprise IT shared services operations. This role encompasses day-to-day cloud infrastructure management, incident resolution, and the design and integration of cloud computing solutions within a federal Common Operating Environment. The ideal candidate brings foundational-to-intermediate cloud computing knowledge and a desire to grow within a fast-paced enterprise IT environment.

Key Responsibilities
  • Manage and administer cloud infrastructure supporting enterprise shared services on a day-to-day basis
  • Resolve Incident tickets related to cloud services in accordance with SLA requirements, analyzing contributing factors of varying complexity
  • Design and plan cloud computing solutions aligned with enterprise architecture standards
  • Integrate cloud services with existing on-premises and hybrid infrastructure
  • Monitor cloud resource utilization, report anomalies, and recommend scaling adjustments to senior engineers
  • Assist in provisioning, configuring, and maintaining cloud resources per established procedures
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to troubleshoot and resolve cloud service disruptions
  • Document incident resolutions, technical solutions, runbooks, and standard operating procedures
  • Contribute to the team knowledge base and continuous improvement initiatives
Qualifications

Required

  • 0–3 years of experience in cloud computing, including designing, planning, or integrating cloud systems
  • Knowledge of cloud computing concepts, architectures, and service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)
  • Cloud computing skills in at least one major platform (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  • Understanding of IT service management frameworks (ITIL fundamentals)
  • Familiarity with cloud networking, storage, and compute services
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a federal public trust clearance

Preferred

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
  • AWS Cloud Practitioner, Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900), or equivalent foundational certification
  • AWS Solutions Architect Associate, Azure Administrator (AZ-104), or equivalent associate-level certification
  • Experience with Infrastructure as Code tools (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Familiarity with ticketing systems (ServiceNow, Remedy)
  • Familiarity with federal compliance frameworks (FedRAMP, FISMA)
  • ITIL Foundation certification or CompTIA Cloud+
  • Prior exposure to federal IT environments

Changeis, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or veteran status.
