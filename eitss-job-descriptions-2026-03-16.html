Cloud Engineer – Junior | EITSS Support | DOT Junior
Position Overview

Changeis, Inc. is seeking a Junior Cloud Engineer to support the Enterprise IT Shared Services (EITSS) contract at the U.S. Department of Transportation / FHWA. This entry-level role focuses on day-to-day cloud infrastructure management and incident resolution within DOT’s Common Operating Environment. The ideal candidate brings foundational cloud computing knowledge and a willingness to grow within a fast-paced federal IT environment.

Key Responsibilities
  • Manage and administer cloud infrastructure supporting DOT’s Common Operating Environment on a day-to-day basis
  • Resolve Incident tickets related to cloud services in accordance with SLA requirements
  • Monitor cloud resource utilization and report anomalies to senior engineers
  • Provide technical solutions to problems of basic scope and complexity involving one or two factors
  • Assist in provisioning, configuring, and maintaining cloud resources per established procedures
  • Document incident resolutions and contribute to the team knowledge base
Qualifications

Required

  • Knowledge of cloud computing concepts, architectures, and service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)
  • Basic cloud computing skills in at least one major platform (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  • Understanding of IT service management frameworks (ITIL fundamentals)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOT public trust clearance

Preferred

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field
  • AWS Cloud Practitioner, Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900), or equivalent foundational cert
  • Familiarity with ticketing systems (ServiceNow, Remedy)
  • Exposure to federal IT environments or DOT/FHWA systems
  • CompTIA Cloud+ or equivalent

This is an opening in support of the EITSS contract at the U.S. Department of Transportation / FHWA.

  • Develop and conduct testing of cloud and virtualization systems across IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS models

  • Lead systems engineering activities: Concept of Operations, Requirements Definition, System Architecting, System Analysis & Design, Interface and Data Architectures, V&V, System Integration, and Operations
  • Conduct functional analysis, timeline analysis, detailed trade studies, requirements allocation, and interface definition studies
  • Perform Performance Analysis, Lifecycle Cost Analysis, Decision Analysis, and Physical Configuration Analysis
  • Provide daily supervision and direction to cloud engineering support staff
  • Advise DOT leadership on cloud strategy, migration planning, and technology roadmap
Qualifications

Required

  • Minimum 7 years managing cloud environments
  • Extensive skills in cloud provider selection, implementation, data migration, and capacity planning
  • At least 1 Associate or Professional-level cloud certification
  • Proven experience in systems engineering lifecycle activities
  • Supervisory or team lead experience
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a DOT public trust clearance

Preferred

  • Bachelor’s in CS or Systems Engineering (Master’s preferred)
  • Multiple cloud certifications across providers or specialties
  • Federal acquisition and IT governance experience (FITARA, TBM)
