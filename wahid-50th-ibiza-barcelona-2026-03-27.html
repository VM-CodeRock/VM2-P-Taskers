3 5-Day A La Carte Itinerary — Mix & Match
✉ Deep Dive ▼
Structure: 2 Nights Barcelona → 2 Nights Ibiza

Days 1–2: Barcelona (arrive Thu night, explore Fri + Sat) → Day 3: Fly to Ibiza (Sat afternoon, 1hr Vueling) → Days 3–4: Ibiza (Sat night + Sun full day/night) → Day 5: Fly home (Mon eve). Each day has Options A/B/C so the group can pick their pace.

Day 1 Arrive Barcelona — Gothic Quarter & Night Awakening Barcelona
10:00–14:00

Arrive + Hotel Check-In

IAD → BCN overnight (depart Thu ~7pm, arrive Fri ~9am). Check into hotel, freshen up. Light terrace brunch at the hotel or a nearby café. Suggested: brunch at Boca Grande on Passatge de la Concepció — elegant seafood and socializing spot beloved by Barcelona's elite.

14:00–17:00

Afternoon — City Exploration (A La Carte)

Option A — Culture & Architecture

Private walking tour of the Gothic Quarter, La Barceloneta, and Eixample Modernisme. Visit Sagrada Família exterior (no queue with private guide). ~€400 for private guide for group.

Option B — Chill & Rooftop Drinks

Skip the museums. Settle into the W Barcelona WAVE rooftop bar overlooking the Mediterranean. Order gin tonics and survey the coastline. Pre-dinner cocktail session at Sips Drinkery House — ranked #3 World's 50 Best Bars, founded by Simone Caporale. Pure mixology theater.

Option C — Port Olímpic & Beach Walk

Stroll Barceloneta Beach, grab cold beers at a chiringuito, finish at the Port Olímpic marina admiring superyachts. Very chill recovery vibe after the overnight flight.

20:00–23:00

Dinner — Michelin Opening Night (A La Carte)

Option A — Crown Jewel: Disfrutar ★★★

Disfrutar (C. de Villarroel, 163) — Recently voted #1 restaurant in the world. Three Michelin Stars. Tasting menu ~€280/person. Avant-garde, theatrical, electric. Book 2–3 months in advance. Michelin Spain 2026 Guide.

Option B — Seafood Focus: Lasarte ★★★ or Enoteca Paco Perez ★★

Lasarte (C/ de Mallorca, 259) — 3 stars, inside the Monument Hotel. Chef Martín Berasategui's flagship. Or Enoteca Paco Perez ★★ (Hotel Arts, waterfront) — focused entirely on sea, mountains, garden. Stunning views. ~€200–250/pp with wine pairing. Barcelona Michelin guide.

Option C — Modern Tapas Crawl (Eixample)

Start at Mont Bar ★★ (C/ de la Diputació, 220) for snacks and an incredible wine list, then walk to Slow & Low ★ for seafood-forward creative plates. Less formal, more social. Perfect if the group is jet-lagged and wants to graze.

23:00–Late

Nightcap — Barcelona's Best Cocktail Bars

Paradiso (C. Rera Palau, 4) — The world-famous speakeasy in El Born. Hidden entrance behind a refrigerator in a pastrami shop. Ranked #4 World's 50 Best Bars 2022. Or: Dry Martini (Eixample) — 100+ martini variations, leather armchairs, old-world sophistication since 1978. Barcelona cocktail bar guide.

Day 2 Golf Morning + Tapas + Barcelona Night Barcelona
07:30–14:00

Golf — Championship Course (A La Carte)

Option A — Camiral (Spain's #1 Course)

Camiral Golf & Wellness — Ranked #1 golf course in Spain, Europe top 10, Spain's Ryder Cup candidate. Stadium Course. 55 min from Barcelona. Pristine fairways, 5-star clubhouse. Green fee: ~€180–250/pp. Add private buggy and caddie. barcelonagolf.com.

Option B — Real Club de Golf El Prat (Historic, Greg Norman Design)

Only 25 min from city centre. 45 holes, has hosted the Spanish Open 10 times. Greg Norman design. Private club that welcomes visitors with advance booking. Green fee: ~€150–200/pp. OnTee Barcelona golf guide.

Option C — Skip Golf, Explore Sitges

30-min train to the beautiful coastal town of Sitges. Beach walk, waterfront café, lunch at a seafood restaurant with terrace views. Perfect if the group's golf instincts are lower priority.

14:30–17:30

Post-Golf Lunch & Afternoon

Return to Barcelona. Late lunch at Estimar (C/ de Sant Antoni dels Sombrerers, 3) — arguably Barcelona's best seafood restaurant, 1 Michelin Star, wild fish and shellfish treated with reverence. Or: classic paella and cold rosé at a terrace in Barceloneta. Rest at hotel before evening.

19:00–22:00

Mixology Experience + Dinner (Sat Night)

Option A — Private Mixology Masterclass

Book a private 2-hour mixology masterclass at a premium venue in Eixample — a personal bartender guides the group through making 5+ signature cocktails, explaining spirits and techniques. Tailored to Wahid's love of drinks/mixology. ~€150–200/pp. Pairs perfectly as a birthday activity.

Option B — Dinner: ABaC ★★★

ABaC (Av. del Tibidabo, 1) — Jordi Cruz's 3-star restaurant inside a boutique hotel. 6 total Michelin stars across his restaurants. Tasting menu ~€220–260/pp. Stunning terrace in the Sarrià hills above the city.

23:00–Late

Barcelona Nightlife

Barcelona's clubs don't fill until 1–2am. Pre-drinks at Soho House Barcelona (rooftop pool terrace). Then: Sala Apolo for live DJs in a converted music hall, or Opium Barcelona (club on the beachfront, electronic/commercial fusion), or Razzmatazz (5 rooms, iconic venue, various genres simultaneously). Barcelona nights end at sunrise — pace accordingly.

Day 3 Barcelona Farewell → Fly to Ibiza → Arrive & Settle In Barcelona → Ibiza
10:00–13:00

Late Brunch & Hotel Check-Out

Sleep in. Hotel brunch. Pack. Check out by noon. If staying near Port Vell — quick visit to Mercat de la Boqueria or the Gothic Quarter for one final Barcelona wander.

13:30–15:30

Fly BCN → IBZ

Vueling Airlines — multiple daily flights, 1hr 5min flight. Recommended departure: 13:30 or 14:35 from BCN. Tickets ~€40–120/pp depending on booking window. BCN to IBZ schedule. Arrive Ibiza ~15:30. Private transfer from IBZ airport to hotel (~€60–80 for group).

16:00–19:00

Ibiza Hotel Check-In + Beach

Check into luxury hotel in Ibiza Town or Playa d'en Bossa. Drop bags, change. Head directly to the beach or hotel pool. First gin tonic in Ibiza at the pool bar. The island starts working its magic.

19:00–21:00

Sunset — Ibiza's Iconic Pre-Party (A La Carte)

Option A — Sunset Catamaran

Private sunset catamaran charter around the southern coast of Ibiza. Captain, crew, open bar. Watch the sun sink over Es Vedrà rock from the water. ~€1,500–2,500 for 2-3 hours for group. Charters Eleven Ibiza.

Option B — Sunset Dinner at Es Boldadó

Es Boldadó — Iconic cliffside seafood restaurant with the most spectacular views of Es Vedrà rock. Book the terrace table at sunset. Fresh grilled fish, cold white wine, jaw-dropping views. A true Ibiza ritual.

Option C — Café del Mar (Classic)

The original Ibiza sunset bar in San Antonio. Watch the legendary Ibiza sunset with the full crowd, ambient music, and cold drinks. Part of the authentic Ibiza experience every first-timer must do.

21:30–00:00

Ibiza Night One — Dinner & First Club

Dinner at La Gaia ★ (Ibiza Gran Hotel) — Chef Óscar Molina's "Mediterranean Kaiseki" — Balearic marine ecosystems on a plate. Exquisite seafood. 1 Michelin Star. Ibiza 2026 Michelin guide. Then: depending on the night of the week, hit the first club. Saturday: Skepta + Black Coffee at Hï Ibiza. Sunday: Solomun +1 at Pacha or Carl Cox at UNVRS.

Day 4 Ibiza Full Day — Beach Club + Golf + Ultimate Night Ibiza
12:00–16:00

Midday — Beach Club Day (A La Carte)

Option A — Blue Marlin Ibiza (Celebrity Scene)

Blue Marlin Ibiza at Cala Jondal — Ibiza's most glamorous beach club. Celebrity magnet, VIP loungers, excellent food (surf 'n' turf, sashimi, pasta), weekly DJ sets. Book VIP section in advance. Pete Tong opened the 2026 season. Ibiza beach club guide.

Option B — Nikki Beach or Nassau Beach Club

Nikki Beach (near Santa Eulalia) — All-white cabanas, international luxury brand, events and live music. Or: Nassau Beach Club on Playa d'en Bossa — gastronomic excellence, Teppanyaki, Japanese-Mediterranean fusion, great music and shows. Ibiza Spotlight spring openings.

Option C — Morning Golf: Golf de Ibiza

Golf de Ibiza — The island's primary 18-hole course in a rugged valley near Roca Llisa, 7km from Ibiza Town. Sea breeze, mountain backdrops, spectacular views. Or: the 9-hole Roca Llisa course for a quick round before beach time. golfibiza.com.

16:00–18:30

Private Yacht Charter

Book a luxury crewed yacht (motor or sailing) for a 2.5-hour afternoon cruise around the southern coastline. Anchored swim stops at crystal clear coves, champagne on deck, fresh ceviche brought onboard. This is the birthday moment — toast Wahid at sea. Full-day luxury yacht charter: €2,500–9,000. Half-day private charter (4 hrs, catamaran): €1,500–3,000 for the group. Viravira catamaran charter Ibiza, YachtCharterFleet.

19:30–22:00

Wahid's Birthday Dinner — The Main Event

Option A — Private Chef at Villa

Hire a Michelin-trained private chef to cook an exclusive birthday dinner in a rented villa or hotel terrace. Chef Cesar Gastardelli (Eivichefs) or similar — tailored seafood omakase menu, 7 courses, wine pairing, formal service. ~€800–1,500 for group. Truly intimate and memorable. Eivichefs, ChefMaison Ibiza.

Option B — Omakase by Walt ★ (The Rarest Table)

Omakase by Walt (Ibiza Town) — Tiny, ultra-exclusive Japanese omakase with only a handful of seats. Daily menu based on the day's best catch. Edomae-style sushi using local Ibizan seafood. 1 Michelin Star 2026. Book months in advance. Wahid's seafood + Michelin fantasy in one seat. Diario de Ibiza Michelin 2026.

Option C — 1742 (Ultra-Exclusive Palace Dining)

1742 — Sensory dining in a restored palace in the heart of Dalt Vila (Ibiza Old Town). Ultra-exclusive. A theatrical progression through courses in a spectacular historical setting. Unforgettable for a milestone birthday.

23:30–Sunrise

Wahid's Birthday Night — The Grand Finale

VIP Table: Solomun +1 at Pacha Ibiza (Sunday) — Book a reserved VIP table. Solomun plays an 8-hour marathon set. This is the peak of Ibiza music culture: sophisticated, euphoric, and deeply musical. The entire club is built for this moment. Champagne service at the table throughout. Stay until 6am. pacha.com.
