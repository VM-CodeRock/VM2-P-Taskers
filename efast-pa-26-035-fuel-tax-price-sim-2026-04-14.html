Pricing Strategy & Recommendation

Overall Recommendation: Bid at Competitive Scenario (Changeis Ceiling Rates) Submit the proposal using Changeis's actual PY17 ceiling rates for the base year and PY18 ceiling rates for the first option year. Do not reduce rates below ceiling for this particular task — the evaluation values technical quality significantly over price, and the modest premium over the field median ($7.88 more than average for PM) is well within acceptable range for a qualified SDB/EDWOSB proposer. The pricing advantage of being a verified EDWOSB (strong agency commitment to contracting with this type of business) outweighs the need to compete purely on price.
